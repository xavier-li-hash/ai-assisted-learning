## Part 0：机考草稿纸策略（先读这里）

机考**会提供铅笔+草稿纸**，可以手写，只是不能在屏幕上标注。

Matching题的草稿纸用法（30秒预读时间内完成）：

```
选项字母 + 1-2个关键词（抽象层）

例：
A = historical time
B = different period
C = similar to another film
D = only part of play
E = ...
```

不要抄整句选项，只写**最能区分各选项的核心概念**。
听音频时眼睛扫草稿纸，而不是回头看屏幕。

### A组：Day 3 原题复盘（已知答案，理解转化逻辑）

| 原文说的 | 选项的描述 | 转化类型 |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| makes you see what life was like at the time of the play | clearly shows the **historical period** | 具象体验 → 抽象概念 |
| moves the action into the present day | presents the play in a **different period** from the original | 具体操作 → 结果描述 |
| more like a typical action hero move | too **similar to another kind** of film | 举例说明 → 类别判断 |
| a blend of a documentary with a few scenes from the play | contains only **parts** of the play | 成分描述 → 整体属性 |

**规律总结（自己填）：**
- 最常见的转化方向是：___________________
- 选项比原文更___________________（具体/抽象）

### B组：新例子——自己做转化（答案在最下方）

下面是模拟原文句子，请把它转化成"选项级别"的简短描述（4-8个词）：

1. 原文：*"The director decided to use all female actors for every role in the play."*
   → 选项应该写：`_________________________________`

2. 原文：*"It focuses on what happens backstage rather than the performance itself."*
   → 选项应该写：`_________________________________`

3. 原文：*"The special effects were so realistic that the audience couldn't tell what was real."*
   → 选项应该写：`_________________________________`

4. 原文：*"They cut about half the scenes from the original script to make it shorter."*
   → 选项应该写：`_________________________________`

> 参考答案（做完再看）：
> 1. features an unconventional casting choice / uses non-traditional cast
> 2. focuses on behind-the-scenes aspects
> 3. makes it difficult to distinguish reality from fiction
> 4. significantly shortened from the original

## 错误原因分类

- `paraphrase` — 听懂原文但没对应上选项的抽象表达
- `速度` — 语速太快跟不上
- `生词` — 不认识这个词
- `预读不足` — 30秒内没建立好选项关键词
