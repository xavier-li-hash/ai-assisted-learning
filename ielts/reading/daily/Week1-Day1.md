# 阅读 Week 1：T/F/NG 专项训练

> 本周重点：True / False / Not Given 题型
> 每天做1篇或半篇，专注这个题型

---

## Week 1 训练重点

**T/F/NG 的判断逻辑：**

```
True    = 题目内容与原文信息一致（可能用了同义词）
False   = 题目内容与原文矛盾（原文说A，题目说not A）
NG      = 原文完全没有提到这个信息（不是感觉上，是真的没有）
```

---

## 练习题（模拟短文）

阅读以下短文，回答 T/F/NG 题：

---

**短文：**

> Urban farming has become increasingly popular in many cities around the world.
> Residents in cities like New York and Tokyo have started growing vegetables on rooftops
> and in community gardens. Proponents argue that urban farming reduces food transportation
> costs and provides fresher produce for local communities. However, critics point out that
> the amount of food produced is often too small to have a meaningful impact on a city's
> overall food supply. Some cities have introduced financial incentives to encourage
> more residents to participate in urban farming programmes.

---

**题目（判断 True / False / Not Given）：**

1. Urban farming is popular in cities including New York and Tokyo.
   答：__T____

2. Urban farming always saves money for residents.
   答：__NG____

3. Critics believe urban farming cannot significantly contribute to a city's food supply.
   答：___T___

4. The quality of food produced by urban farming is better than food from supermarkets.
   答：___NG___

5. Some governments offer financial support to promote urban farming.
   答：___T___

---

## 答案与解析

1. **True** — 原文明确提到 New York and Tokyo
2. **Not Given** — 原文说减少运输成本，但没说"always saves money for residents"
3. **True** — "too small to have a meaningful impact" = cannot significantly contribute
4. **Not Given** — 原文说"fresher"，但没有与超市比较
5. **True** — "financial incentives" = financial support
