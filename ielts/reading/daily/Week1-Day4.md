## 一、为什么同义替换是阅读最大的坑

IELTS 阅读的出题逻辑：
- 题目**几乎不直接抄原文词**
- 必须用另一个词/短语"转述"原文意思
- 你需要识别：`题目词 → 原文词` 的映射关系

**常见替换方式：**

| 替换类型 | 例子 |
|---------|------|
| 同义词 | beneficial → advantageous / helpful |
| 上位词 | oak tree → plant / species |
| 下位词 | transportation → flights |
| 动词转名词 | discover → discovery / finding |
| 主动转被动 | scientists developed → was developed by scientists |
| 肯定转否定 | impossible → cannot be done |
| 抽象转具体 | made progress → achieved a 20% increase |

| study / research | investigation / examination / analysis / survey |
| find / discover | reveal / identify / demonstrate / show / indicate |

| prove / confirm | verify / validate / establish / demonstrate |
| cause | result in / lead to / contribute to / give rise to |
| affect | influence / impact / alter / change |

**练习 1：** 用以上替换词造句，保持原意不变：

原句：`Scientists studied the effect of light on plant growth.`
改写（至少2种）：
- A: The influence of light on plant growth was analysised by scientists.
- B: Scientists analysised the impact of light on plant growth.

## 三、真题同义替换练习（模拟题）

阅读以下段落，完成替换识别练习：

---

**段落：**

> The rapid expansion of urban areas has created significant challenges for city planners.
> Traffic congestion has worsened considerably, and air quality has deteriorated in many
> metropolitan regions. Some municipalities have introduced low-emission zones to restrict
> the movement of heavily polluting vehicles. Advocates of these measures argue that the
> long-term health benefits for residents far outweigh the short-term inconvenience caused
> to drivers.

---

**题目（T/F/NG）：**

1. The growth of cities has caused problems for those responsible for urban development.

   - 题目词 "growth" → 原文词：______expansion_________
   - 题目词 "problems" → 原文词：_______challenges________
   - 题目词 "those responsible for urban development" → 原文词：______city planners_________
   - 我的答案：____TRUE___ | 正确答案：**True**

2. Air pollution has increased in all major cities.

   - 关键对比："all major cities" vs 原文：______in many
> metropolitan regions_________
   - 我的答案：_______ | 正确答案：**False**（原文说 many，不是 all）

3. Drivers have complained about the new restrictions.

   - 原文是否提到司机的反应？________没有_______
   - 我的答案：__NG_____ | 正确答案：**Not Given**

4. Supporters of emission zones believe health gains justify the disruption to traffic.

   - 题目词 "supporters" → 原文词：______Advocates_________
   - 题目词 "health gains" → 原文词：_____benefits for__________
   - 题目词 "justify" → 原文词：________far outweigh_______
   - 我的答案：_______ | 正确答案：**True**

## 四、今日生词记忆（10个）
