# 阅读 Week 1 D5：长难句拆解专项

> 目标：面对复杂句子不慌，快速找到"谁做了什么"
> 用时：30–40 分钟
> 核心技能：主干提取 + 从句判断 + 代词指代追踪

---

## 一、长难句的3个核心问题

做阅读时看到长句，最容易犯的错：

1. **主语弄错** — 定语从句修饰名词，但误以为从句主语就是主句主语
2. **代词指代追踪失败** — it / they / this / which 指代的是哪个名词？
3. **否定叠加** — "not … without …" / "few … unless …" 等双重否定

---

## 二、句子拆解方法（3步法）

**Step 1 — 找主干：** 找到主语（S）+ 谓语（V）+ 宾语（O）
**Step 2 — 括号法：** 把定语从句、状语从句、插入语用括号括起来，先不看
**Step 3 — 代词追踪：** 每个 it/they/this/which，往前找对应名词

### 句子 1

```
The technology, which had been developed over several decades by a team of researchers
who had largely been ignored by the mainstream scientific community, finally attracted
significant attention when it was demonstrated to be effective in controlled trials.
```

**拆解：**

| 成分 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 主语（S） | The technology |
| 谓语（V） | attracted |
| 宾语（O） | significant attention |
| 主句核心意思 | 这项技术终于引起了关注 |
| 插入成分 | which had been developed over several decades by a team of researchers who had largely been ignored... |
| 触发条件 | when it was demonstrated to be effective in controlled trials |

**代词指代：** "it" → _______________（答：the technology）

**一句话概括原文意思：** _______________________________________________

### 句子 2

```
Although many scientists initially dismissed the findings as inconclusive, subsequent
studies not only confirmed the original results but also revealed previously unknown
mechanisms that could explain the observed phenomenon.
```

**拆解：**

| 成分 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 让步状语 | Although many scientists … as inconclusive |
| 主语（S） | subsequent studies |
| 谓语（V） | confirmed … revealed |
| 关键修饰 | not only … but also |
| 核心意思 | |

**判断题练习：**

> 题目：Scientists were unanimous in accepting the initial findings.

### 句子 3（双重否定）

```
It is not uncommon for researchers to find that results which appear straightforward
in laboratory conditions cannot easily be replicated in real-world settings.
```

**双重否定识别：**
- "not uncommon" = _______________（答：common，常见的）
- 整句意思：_______________________________________________

**判断题练习：**

> 题目：Laboratory results are always easily reproduced outside the laboratory.
> 答：_______ （True / False / Not Given）

---

### 句子 4（that/which 套叠）

```
The committee's report, which was published three years after the original inquiry
had concluded, recommended that the policy, which critics had long argued was
fundamentally flawed, should be revised rather than abandoned entirely.
```

**拆解步骤：**

先去掉所有 which 从句，主干是：
```
The committee's report recommended that the policy should be revised rather than abandoned.
```

**填空：**
- 报告建议：_______ the policy（修订 还是 废除？）
- 批评者认为这个政策：_______
- 报告发布于调查结束后：_______ 年

### 句子 5（代词挑战）

```
While the original settlers had relied on traditional farming methods passed down
through generations, their descendants gradually adopted new techniques introduced
by traders from neighbouring regions, though they did not entirely abandon the
practices their ancestors had developed.
```

**代词追踪：**
- "they" (第二个) → 指：_______________
- "their ancestors" → 指：_______________

**判断题练习：**

> 题目：The descendants completely replaced traditional practices with new ones.
> 答：_______ （True / False / Not Given）
> 关键词对比："completely replaced" vs 原文：_______________

## 四、今日生词记忆（10个）

| 词/短语 | 词性 | 含义 | 同义词 | 常见搭配 |

| replicate | v | 复制，重复（实验）| reproduce, duplicate, copy | replicate results / findings |

## 五、今日抽象练习：逻辑关系识别

> 阅读句子，判断两件事之间的逻辑关系

> *The introduction of standardised testing led to widespread criticism from educators,
> who argued that it placed too much emphasis on memorisation at the expense of
> creative thinking.*

| 事件 A | 逻辑关系 | 事件 B |
|--------|---------|--------|
| standardised testing | 导致（caused） | 教育者的批评 |
| 过度强调记忆 | ___（代价是） | ___________减少 |

**判断：** 教育者对标准化测试持什么态度？
- [ ] 支持
- [ ] 反对
- [ ] 态度不明确
