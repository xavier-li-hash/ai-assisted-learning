## 逻辑链条训练（3分钟）

> **本周核心习惯：提出一个观点之后，不能停——往下走三步。**
>
> 公式：**观点 → 原因（Because...）→ 例子（For example, last week I...）→ 影响（That's why / This means...）**
>
> 这就是"不说抽象话"的训练。

大声说出以下两个链条，横线处填入你自己的真实内容：

**链条 A：**
```
起始句（太模糊）：I like living here.
→ 原因：This is mainly because there are plenty of _______ nearby, like _______.
→ 例子：For example, just last _______, I _______ in my neighbourhood.
→ 影响：That's why I feel _______, and I can't imagine living somewhere _______.
```

This is mainly because there are plenty of recreational facilities nearby, like public parks and jogging tracks.

例子： For example, just last weekend, I went for a long run and enjoyed the fresh air in my neighbourhood.

影响： That's why I feel refreshed and energetic, and I can't imagine living somewhere with too much concrete and noise.

**链条 B：**
```
起始句（太模糊）：My job is interesting.
→ 原因：This is because I often get to _______, which involves _______.
→ 例子：For instance, just recently I had to ___refrect the recommendation system to improve its performance____, which took about ___two days to analyse the bottleneck where it is and design a sophisticated solution to optimise____.
→ 影响：So even though it can be ___challengeable____, I still find it ___that make me fullfill____.
```

> 感受一下：加了三步之后，回答立刻有内容了。这就是雅思7分口语和6分的差距。

原因： This is because I often get to tackle complex technical problems, which involves optimizing large-scale data algorithms.

例子： For instance, just recently I had to refactor the recommendation system to improve its performance, which took about two days to analyze the bottleneck and design a sophisticated solution to optimize it.

影响： So even though it can be challenging, I still find it intellectually fulfilling.

## 今日题目：关于你自己（5题）

用20分钟，每题说2–3句话，然后进下一题。

**题1：住所**
> Where do you live? Do you like living there?

提示：说城市名 + 一个你喜欢/不喜欢的理由

---

**题2：工作或学习**
> Are you working or studying at the moment?

提示：说你在做什么 + 一个关于它的感受

---

**题3：家乡**
> What is your hometown like?

提示：描述一个具体特点（天气/食物/大小/节奏）

My hometown is located in the south part of China, which is tropicanal area. Furthermore, there are more than more million 
Hakka people living there, who are imigrate from the center of China and across more than one thousand years.

My hometown is located in southern China, which has a tropical climate. What's really interesting is that it's home to millions of Hakka people. Our ancestors actually immigrated from central China over a thousand years ago, bringing a unique culture to the region.

原因： This is mainly because there is a unique Hakka culture there, like traditional walled houses and special cuisine.

例子： For example, just last time I visited, I tasted the authentic 'Stuffed Tofu' (酿豆腐) in my neighbourhood.

影响： That's why I feel proud of my roots, and I can't imagine living somewhere without such a deep sense of history.

---

**题4：日常习惯**
> What do you usually do in the evenings?

提示：说一个具体活动 + 为什么

---

**题5：最喜欢的季节**
> What is your favourite season? Why?

提示：季节名 + 两个原因（可以很简单）

---

## 示例参考（题1）

差的回答（1句，没扩展）：
> I live in Shanghai.

好的回答（3句，有细节）：
> I live in Shanghai, which is a really busy city.
> I like it because there are so many restaurants and things to do.
> However, the traffic can be quite stressful sometimes.
