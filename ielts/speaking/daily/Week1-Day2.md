I really enjoy spending my free time *outdoors*, especially at weekends.
One hobby I've had for a long time is reading every night *before bed*.
It's not something I'm particularly *good* at, but I find it very *relaxing*.

## 逻辑链条训练（3分钟）

> **昨天的链条公式今天继续练。今天重点：理由要"真实"，例子要"有细节"（时间/地点/数字）。**

大声说出以下链条，横线处填入你自己的真实内容：

**链条 A — 兴趣爱好：**
```
起始句（太模糊）：I enjoy [a hobby].
→ 原因：I enjoy __reading_____ because it helps me ___switch off____, especially after ___long hours working____.
→ 例子：For example, last ___night____ I ____read___ for about ___2____ hours and I felt ___relaxed____.
→ 影响：So whenever I feel __exausted_____, the first thing I do is ___reading____.
```

**链条 B — 对比偏好：**
```
起始句（太模糊）：I prefer [X] over [Y].
→ 原因：I prefer ___Chinese food____ over ___Malaysia food____ mainly because ___I have had Chinese food for 30 years since I was birth____.
→ 例子：For instance, ___last night____ I tried __coconut rice_____, but I ended up _with disappointment_____.
→ 影响：That experience made me realise that ___food from hometown____ just suits me better.
```

---

## 今日核心公式

```
观点（1句） → 理由（1句） → 例子或细节（1句）
```

示例：
> 问：Do you enjoy reading?
> 答：Yes, I really enjoy reading.
>     It helps me learn new things and escape from daily stress.
>     For example, I'm currently reading a book about psychology.
