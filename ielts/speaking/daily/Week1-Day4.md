## 热身（2分钟）

大声读出以下句子，感受"思考中"的语气（不要读得太流畅）：

```
Hmm, let me think about that for a second...
Actually, the first thing that came to mind was...
I suppose what I enjoy most about it is...
Looking back, I think what made it special was...
So overall, I'd probably say it means quite a lot to me.
```

---

## Part 1 — 家乡与居住环境（共3题，每题30–45秒）

> 框架：**AREA** — Answer → Reason → Evidence → Add
> 目标：每题用"我能想到的真实细节"填满30秒，不要超过45秒

---

**题1：**
> Where did you grow up? What was it like?

提示：城市名 + 一个具体特点（气候/文化/节奏）+ 你对它的感受

---

**题2：**
> Do you prefer living in a big city or a smaller town?

提示：直接表态 + 一个原因 + 你自己生活里的一个例子

---

**题3：**
> Has your neighbourhood changed much since you were a child?

提示：说一个具体的变化（某个地方消失了/新建了什么）+ 你的感受

---

## Part 2 — 描述卡片（准备1分钟，说1–1.5分钟）

> **Describe a place in your hometown that is special to you.**
>
> You should say:
> - Where it is
> - What it looks like
> - What memories you have of it
> - And explain why it is special to you

**准备提示（1分钟内在脑里想好这四点，不要写句子）：**

```
S (Set up):  "The place I want to describe is..."
T (Time):    "I first remember going there when I was..."
O (Observe): "What I remember most is the..." （用感官细节）
R (Reflect): "Looking back, what made it special was..."
Y (You now): "Even now, whenever I think about it..."
```

**Week 1说法要求**：能说完1分钟，把4个要点都覆盖到就算完成。不要求完美。

---

## Part 3 — 抽象讨论（共3题，每题说45秒）

> 框架：**VIEW** — Voice → Illustrate → Examine → Wrap
> Week 1 的目标：能说出立场 + 一个理由 + 一个例子（不强求说到"另一面"）

---

**题1：**
> Do you think people are losing a sense of connection to their hometowns? Why?

思考提示：年轻人迁移/城市化/虚拟社区

---

**题2：**
> Should governments invest more in developing rural areas, or focus on cities?

思考提示：平衡发展 / 人口分布 / 你家乡的情况

---

**题3：**
> How important is it for people to feel proud of where they come from?

思考提示：身份认同 / 文化传承 / 你对客家文化的感受
