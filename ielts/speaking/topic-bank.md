# 雅思口语话题题库

> 按话题分类，每个话题包含 Part 1（3题）、Part 2（1张卡片）、Part 3（3题）
> 每次练习围绕**同一话题**做完全套，保持内容连贯

## T1：个人与日常习惯

### Part 1（每题说30–45秒）

1. What time do you usually wake up? Do you consider yourself a morning person?
2. Do you have any habits you do every day without thinking? Are they good or bad habits?
3. How do you usually spend your evenings after work or study?

### Part 2（说1.5–2分钟）

> **Describe a daily routine that you enjoy.**
> You should say:
> - What it is
> - When you started doing it
> - What you do during this routine
> - And explain why you enjoy it

### Part 3（每题说45秒–1.5分钟）

1. Do you think people's daily routines have changed a lot compared to 20 years ago? Why?
2. Some people say a strict routine is essential for success. Do you agree?
3. How does a person's lifestyle reflect their values?

## T2：家乡与居住环境

### Part 1

1. Where did you grow up? What was it like?
2. Do you prefer living in a big city or a smaller town?
3. Has your neighbourhood changed much since you were a child?

### Part 2

> **Describe a place in your hometown that is special to you.**
> You should say:
> - Where it is
> - What it looks like
> - What memories you have of it
> - And explain why it is special to you

### Part 3

1. Do you think people are losing a sense of connection to their hometowns? Why?
2. Should governments invest more in developing rural areas, or focus on cities?
3. How important is it for people to feel proud of where they come from?

---

## T3：工作与职业

### Part 1

1. What do you do for work? Do you enjoy it?
2. What's the most challenging part of your job?
3. Would you ever consider changing careers? Why or why not?

### Part 2

> **Describe a job you think is particularly important for society.**
> You should say:
> - What the job is
> - What people in this job do
> - Why you think it matters
> - And explain whether you would want to do this job

### Part 3

1. Do you think people today have more career choices than previous generations?
2. Is it more important to have a job you love or one that pays well?
3. How might automation change the types of jobs available in the future?

## T4：教育与学习

### Part 1

1. Did you enjoy school when you were younger? What was your favourite subject?
2. Are you still learning new things now? What kind of things?
3. Do you think online learning is better or worse than traditional classroom learning?

### Part 2

> **Describe a teacher or lecturer who had a positive influence on you.**
> You should say:
> - Who they were
> - What subject they taught
> - How they taught differently from others
> - And explain how they influenced your life

### Part 3

1. What qualities make a truly excellent teacher?
2. Should education systems focus more on practical skills or academic knowledge?
3. How important is lifelong learning in the modern world?

---

## T5：科技与互联网

### Part 1

1. How much time do you spend on your phone each day? What do you mainly use it for?
2. Are there any apps you rely on heavily? Which ones?
3. Do you think people are too dependent on technology? What about yourself?

### Part 2

> **Describe a piece of technology that has changed your life.**
> You should say:
> - What it is
> - When you first started using it
> - How it changed the way you work or live
> - And explain whether you think this change is mostly positive or negative

### Part 3

1. Has social media improved or worsened the way people communicate?
2. Should governments regulate how technology companies use personal data?
3. Do you think artificial intelligence will eventually replace human workers in most fields?

## T6：健康与运动

### Part 1

1. Do you do any regular exercise? What kind?
2. How important is sleep to you? Do you get enough?
3. Do you pay attention to what you eat? Have your eating habits changed recently?

### Part 2

> **Describe a sport or physical activity you enjoy doing.**
> You should say:
> - What it is
> - How long you have been doing it
> - Who you do it with, if anyone
> - And explain why you enjoy it

### Part 3

1. Do you think modern life makes it harder for people to stay healthy?
2. What role should governments play in encouraging healthy lifestyles?
3. Is mental health given enough attention in society compared to physical health?

---

## T7：娱乐与艺术

### Part 1

1. Do you prefer watching movies at home or in a cinema? Why?
2. Do you listen to music often? What kind?
3. Do you enjoy reading? What do you usually read?

### Part 2

> **Describe a film, book, or song that made a strong impression on you.**
> You should say:
> - What it was
> - When you first experienced it
> - What it was about
> - And explain what made it memorable to you

### Part 3

1. Do you think art and music should be compulsory subjects in school?
2. How has streaming changed the way people consume entertainment?
3. Is it important for a society to preserve traditional forms of art and music?

---

## T8：旅行与文化

### Part 1

1. Do you like travelling? Where have you been recently?
2. Do you prefer travelling alone or with others?
3. When you travel, do you prefer planned itineraries or spontaneous trips?

### Part 2

> **Describe a trip or journey that was memorable for you.**
> You should say:
> - Where you went
> - Who you went with
> - What you did there
> - And explain what made it particularly memorable

### Part 3

1. How has tourism changed local cultures — mostly for better or worse?
2. Do you think people learn more from travelling than from books or schools?
3. Should governments spend more money to promote international cultural exchange?

---

## T9：环境与自然

### Part 1

1. Do you spend much time outdoors? What do you do?
2. Is protecting the environment something you actively think about in daily life?
3. Do you think the weather in your country has changed in recent years?

### Part 2

> **Describe an environmental problem that concerns you.**
> You should say:
> - What the problem is
> - Where it is happening
> - What causes it
> - And explain what you think can be done about it

### Part 3

1. Who is more responsible for environmental protection — individuals or governments?
2. Do you think economic growth and environmental protection can go hand in hand?
3. How has public awareness of environmental issues changed in recent decades?

---

## T10：社会与人际关系

### Part 1

1. Are you more of an introvert or extrovert? How does that affect your friendships?
2. Do you keep in touch with friends from school or your past? How?
3. Has the way people make friends changed with social media?

### Part 2

> **Describe a person in your life who you greatly admire.**
> You should say:
> - Who they are
> - How you know them
> - What qualities they have
> - And explain why you admire them

### Part 3

1. Are close relationships becoming harder to maintain in modern society? Why?
2. Should children be taught more about social skills in school?
3. How has urbanisation affected the sense of community in cities?

## 使用说明

**每日练习方式（约20–25分钟）：**

| 时间 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 0–2分 | 热身：大声说出今天话题的3个相关词 |
| 2–10分 | Part 1：3题各说30–45秒 |
| 10–14分 | Part 2：1分钟准备，然后说1.5–2分钟 |
| 14–22分 | Part 3：3题各说45秒–1.5分钟 |
| 22–25分 | 重说今天最差的1题 |

**话题轮换建议**：
- Week 1: T1 → T2
- Week 2: T3 → T4
- Week 3: T5 → T6
- Week 4: T7 → T8
- Week 5: T9 → T10
- Week 6: 随机抽2个话题
- Week 7–8: 全部话题随机模拟
