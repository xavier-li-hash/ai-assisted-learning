# 雅思口语万能框架

> 核心原则：框架是骨架，内容是血肉。框架让你有组织，个人细节让你不像在背书。

---

## 为什么这套框架不会显得"背诵"？

**考官最怕的信号**：
- 完美的开场白（"That's a very interesting question..."）
- 太流畅、没有思考停顿
- 内容太抽象，没有个人细节
- 回答和问题没有直接关联

**这套框架的设计逻辑**：
- 每个步骤都有"思考触发词"，让回答看起来是实时组织的
- 强制加入具体时间/地点/数字，让内容听起来像真实经历
- 允许自我修正（"Actually, let me rephrase that..."）
- Part 3 故意加入"另一面"，显示真实思考而非单一立场

---

## Part 1 框架：AREA公式（适合30–45秒回答）

> 适用：问你个人习惯、喜好、经历的简短问题

```
A — Answer     直接回答（1句，用口语化表达，不要太正式）
R — Reason     解释原因（"I suppose it's because..." / "mainly because..."）
E — Evidence   举个具体例子（"Like just recently..." / "I remember once..."）
A — Add        加一层质感（对比 / 补充 / 话题延伸）
```

### 关键：让开场显得自然，不像开场白

| 避免（显得背诵） | 改用（显得自然） |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Yes, I do enjoy it very much. | Yeah, I'd say I quite enjoy it actually. |
| I think this is a very important topic. | Hmm, let me think... I suppose I'd say yes. |
| There are many reasons for this. | The main thing for me is probably... |

### Part 1 示例

**问：Do you enjoy cooking?**

```
A: Yeah, I actually enjoy it quite a bit.
R: I suppose it's because cooking feels like a creative outlet for me —
   it's one of the few things where I can completely switch off from work.
E: Like just last weekend, I tried making this Hakka dish from my hometown,
   and it took me about two hours but came out pretty well.
A: Though I have to admit, on weekdays when I'm tired, I'd probably just
   order takeaway instead.
```

**特征**：用了"actually", "I suppose", "just last weekend", "I have to admit" — 听起来是实时在想，不是背的。

## Part 2 框架：STORY法（适合1.5–2分钟独白）

> 适用：Describe a person / place / event / experience / object

```
S — Set up     自然引入话题（用"thinking out loud"的语气）
T — Time       时间锚点（给具体或模糊的时间，让故事有起点）
O — Observe    当时你注意到/感受到什么（感官细节）
R — Reflect    回忆时的想法（用"I think what struck me most was..."）
Y — You now    它对现在的你有什么意义（收尾句，1–2句就够）
```

### 关键：用"刚刚想到"的语气开头

**不自然的开头（显然是背的）：**
> "I would like to talk about a person who has had a significant impact on my life."

**自然的开头（像在现场想）：**
> "Hmm, there are a few things that come to mind, but I think I'll talk about..."
> "Actually, the first thing that popped into my head was..."
> "So the one I want to mention is..."

### Part 2 过渡词库

| 阶段 | 可用表达 |
|------|----------|
| 引入 | "So this is about..." / "I'd like to talk about..." / "The one I want to describe is..." |
| 时间 | "It was about two years ago..." / "Back when I was..." / "I think it was around..." |
| 细节 | "What I remember most is..." / "The thing that really struck me was..." |
| 感受 | "At the time, I felt..." / "Looking back, I think..." |
| 收尾 | "I guess that's why it still sticks with me." / "And yeah, that's the one I wanted to describe." |

### Part 2 示例骨架

**题目：Describe a skill you have learned that you think is useful.**

```
S: So the skill I want to talk about is... actually programming —
   specifically building recommendation systems.

T: I first got into it seriously around... maybe eight years ago,
   when I just joined my first tech company.

O: I remember sitting in front of this massive dataset not knowing
   where to start. The code was just... overwhelming at first.
   But my senior engineer sat with me for an hour and showed me
   the fundamentals, and something just clicked.

R: What really struck me, looking back, was how that one afternoon
   basically changed my career direction. I started staying late
   voluntarily — which says a lot, because I wasn't a workaholic
   by nature.

Y: And now I'm a principal engineer, so I'd say it was probably
   the most useful thing I've ever picked up.
   It's not just a technical skill — it taught me how to think
   through complex problems systematically.
```

## Part 3 框架：VIEW法（适合1–2分钟的论点发展）

> 适用：抽象问题、社会议题、观点辩论类问题

```
V — Voice      给出清晰立场（"Personally, I'd say..." / "My gut feeling is..."）
I — Illustrate 举例说明（"A good example would be..." / "Take [X] for instance..."）
E — Examine    考察另一面（"Of course, some would argue..." / "Having said that..."）
W — Wrap       收尾给出综合判断（"So overall, I think..." / "It's probably more nuanced than..."）
```

### 关键：Part 3 考官想看的是"思考过程"，不是"标准答案"

**加分表达（显示思维深度）：**
- "It depends a lot on the context, I think..."
- "That's actually a tricky one because..."
- "I've gone back and forth on this, but I'd probably say..."
- "I'm not entirely sure, but my instinct is..."

**避免（显得背诵）：**
- "There are both advantages and disadvantages..."
- "On the one hand... On the other hand..."（太教科书式）
- "In conclusion, I believe..."（写作腔）

### Part 3 示例

**问：Do you think technology has made people's lives better or worse?**

```
V: Personally, I'd say better — at least in terms of access to information
   and staying connected. But it's honestly more complicated than that.

I: Like, take remote work as an example. Five years ago, working from
   another country was basically impossible for most people. Now it's
   completely normal. That's changed lives for the better.

E: But then again, there's this argument — which I find pretty compelling —
   that the constant connectivity has made it harder to switch off.
   People check emails at midnight, feel anxious without their phones...
   so in terms of mental health, maybe it's a wash.

W: So I guess my overall view is: the technology itself is neutral —
   it's really about whether we're in control of it or it's in control
   of us. Which varies a lot from person to person.
```

## 通用"救场"表达（出现卡壳时自然过渡）

| 情况 | 可用表达 |
|------|----------|
| 需要时间想 | "That's an interesting one... let me think for a second." / "Hmm, off the top of my head I'd say..." |
| 不确定词汇 | "What's the word... I think it's called..." / "You know that feeling when..." |
| 想补充内容 | "Oh actually, I should mention..." / "And another thing I just thought of is..." |
| 想修正自己 | "Actually, let me rephrase that..." / "Or more precisely..." |
| 不太懂问题 | "So if I'm understanding you correctly, you're asking about...?" |

## 三部分时间感

| 部分 | 目标时长 | 感觉 |
|------|----------|------|
| Part 1 | 30–45秒/题 | 对话式，轻松，有来有往 |
| Part 2 | 1.5–2分钟 | 故事感，有节奏，有起伏 |
| Part 3 | 45秒–1分30秒/题 | 思考感，有立场，有深度 |

---

## 练习时的执行顺序

1. 看到题目 → 先想"我能用什么真实经历来支撑"
2. 套入框架 → 但只用框架决定顺序，不背句子
3. 说出来 → 允许停顿，不允许切换中文
4. 自查 → 有没有具体细节？有没有自然停顿？有没有一直用"very/really"？
