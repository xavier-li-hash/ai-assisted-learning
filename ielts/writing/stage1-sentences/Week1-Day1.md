## 逻辑链条段落练习

> **这是本文件的升级练习。** 上面的句子补全是单句级别；这里训练的是**段落级逻辑链**：
> 给你一个抽象句，你要把它拆成4层，每层填一空，最终写出有血有肉的段落开头。
>
> 公式：**具体化观点 → 原因/机制（Because...）→ 具体例子（有数字/时间/地点）→ 影响/意义（As a result / This means...）**

**练习 P1：**
```
抽象句：Technology has changed the way people study.
（问题：谁？什么技术？怎么变的？）

→ 具体化观点：Technology, particularly _article intellegence_____, has transformed the way __for students to_____ access learning materials.
→ 原因/机制：This is because ___AI can generate high qulity answers for most fields____, which allows learners to ___learn anything by themself_just they want_ without having to __afford_ tuition fee___.
→ 具体例子：For example, platforms such as ___chatGPT____ now enable over ___millions of____ students to ___learn anything imedinately____.
→ 影响：As a result, ____knowledge___ has become far more accessible for ____learning___, regardless of ___how caomplicated it is____.
```

**练习 P2：**
```
抽象句：Cities have many problems.
（问题：哪类城市？哪种问题？多严重？）

→ 具体化观点：One of the most pressing challenges facing __in developing_____ cities is ___environment is damaged dramatically ____.
→ 原因/机制：This problem has largely arisen because __numberous  factories_use_low effetive production methods___, which has led to ___high risk of health____.
→ 具体例子：In ___the last 3 years____, for instance, ____more than millions of___ residents now face __polluted air ____ every day.
→ 影响：Consequently, ___the pullutions from modern production have affected whether he food we take or air we breath ____, which directly undermines ___everyone's health____.
```

> **自查：** 每个空里有没有出现"很好"、"很多"、"非常"这类模糊词？换成具体的数字或描述。

## 逻辑链批改

### P1 批改

> **问题清单：**
> - ❌ "article intellegence" → 拼写错误，应为 **artificial intelligence**
> - ❌ "the way for students to access" → 语法错，应为 "the way **students** access"（way后直接跟从句，不加for）
> - ❌ "high qulity" → 拼写错误 → **high-quality**（注意连字符）
> - ❌ "learn anything by themself just they want" → 严重语序错乱，应为 "learn **whatever they want** independently"
> - ❌ "without having to afford tuition fee" → afford不搭配，应为 "without **paying for** tuition"
> - ❌ "over millions of students" → 数量表达错误，应为 "**millions of** students" 或 "**over a million** students"
> - ❌ "imedinately" → 拼写错误 → **immediately**
> - ❌ "accessible for learning" → 搭配错，应为 "accessible **to learners**"
> - ❌ "regardless of how complicated it is" → 表意不明，应为 "regardless of **their background or location**"

| 分数 | 段落 |
|------|------|
| 6.5 | Technology, particularly **artificial intelligence**, has transformed **the way students** access learning materials. This is because AI can generate high-quality answers across many fields, which allows learners to study **whatever they want** independently without **paying for** tuition. For example, platforms such as ChatGPT now enable **millions of** students to learn **immediately**. As a result, knowledge has become far more accessible **to learners**, regardless of **their background**. |
| 8 | Technology, particularly the rise of artificial intelligence, has fundamentally transformed the way students access and engage with learning materials. This shift has occurred because AI-powered tools can generate accurate, field-specific guidance on demand, enabling learners to pursue self-directed study without the financial burden of formal tuition. For instance, platforms such as ChatGPT now serve millions of students globally, providing instant, personalised explanations across disciplines. As a result, quality education has become considerably more accessible to individuals regardless of their socioeconomic background or geographic location. |

### P2 批改

> **问题清单：**
> - ❌ "challenges facing **in** developing cities" → 多余介词，应为 "challenges **facing** developing cities"
> - ❌ "environment is damaged dramatically" → 名词化更地道，应为 "**severe environmental degradation**"
> - ❌ "numerous factories use low effective production methods" → "low effective"不存在，应为 "**inefficient** production methods"
> - ❌ "high risk of health" → 搭配错，应为 "**serious health risks**"
> - ❌ "more than millions of" → 数量表达错，应为 "**tens of millions of**" 或 "**millions of**"
> - ❌ "whether the food we take or air we breath" → whether...or误用；take→**consume**；breath→**breathe**（名词/动词混淆）；应改为 "**both** the food we consume **and** the air we breathe"

This crisis has largely been driven by the proliferation of factories that continue to rely on outdated and inefficient production processes, generating toxic emissions that pose grave risks to public health. In cities such as Jakarta or Dhaka, for instance, millions of residents are exposed daily to hazardous air pollution levels that far exceed WHO safety thresholds. Consequently, industrial contamination now threatens both the food supply and the air quality that communities depend upon, with measurable consequences for life expectancy and long-term wellbeing. |
