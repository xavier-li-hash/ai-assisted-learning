## 今日语法焦点

**因果句两种写法：**

正向（原因在前）：
> `Because smartphones are widely used, many people spend less time reading books.`

逆向（结果在前）：
> `Many people spend less time reading books because smartphones are so addictive.`

### 一、因果句练习（5句）

**句1**
> ❌ it is so convenient to join different and recreational communities
> ✅ it offers greater access to employment opportunities and recreational facilities
- "join recreational communities" 表达不自然；城市吸引力应说 access to opportunities/facilities
- "different and recreational" 并列逻辑奇怪，different 在这里没有实意

主系表-主谓宾，都是句子，有时候可以换一种句式，来变得更自然，如果某种句式的表达感觉不熟练

**句2**
> ❌ read less books and do less exercises
> ✅ read **fewer** books and get less exercise
- **less/fewer 区分**：可数名词用 fewer（books）；exercise 作"锻炼"时是不可数，不加 s

**句3**
> ❌ bad health
> ✅ serious health problems such as obesity and chronic disease
- "bad health" 太笼统，Task 2 评分要求结果"具体化"（自查清单第2条你自己写了！）

**句4**
> ❌ it is benefitial for students to engage knowledge
> ✅ students are able to acquire knowledge and improve their academic performance at no cost
- 拼写：benefitial → **beneficial**
- 搭配：engage knowledge ❌ → acquire/access knowledge ✅（engage 后接 with/in，不直接接名词）

**句5**
> ❌ He cannot control how much he eat
> ✅ He cannot control how much he **eats**
- 主谓一致：he/she/it + 动词三单 → eats
- 标点建议：用分号代替逗号："He cannot control how much he eats**;** therefore, his weight will increase rapidly."

### 逻辑链段落 7分示例（P2 自由发挥）

**抽象句：Working long hours is harmful.**

> The widespread practice of working long hours poses a serious threat to the physical and psychological well-being of employees, particularly those in high-pressure industries such as technology and finance. This is because prolonged overwork deprives individuals of adequate rest and physical activity, both of which are essential for maintaining good health. Studies conducted by the World Health Organization indicate that people who regularly work more than 55 hours per week are significantly more likely to develop cardiovascular disease and suffer from chronic stress-related conditions. As a result, healthcare systems in many countries are facing mounting pressure to accommodate a growing number of patients whose conditions stem directly from work-related exhaustion.

**逐句分析：**

| 句子功能 | 关键词/技巧 | 对比你的写法 |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| **具体化观点** | `widespread practice of` + `well-being` + `particularly those in` | 你写了 phosilogy → 改为 psychological；widespread 后需要名词核心 |
| **原因/机制** | `deprives ... of` + `both of which are essential` | 你的 which 主语判断错误（应为单数）；"sitting before" → `in front of` |
| **具体例子** | `55 hours per week` + `significantly more likely to` + `cardiovascular disease` | 你写了 "5 times likely"，缺少 more；disease 拼写需注意 |
| **影响** | `facing mounting pressure` + `stem directly from` | 你写了 numberous（拼写）+ "caused by over load working"（不自然） |

**7分段落的核心差距：**
1. **结果词汇**：obesity / cardiovascular disease / chronic stress-related conditions，不说 fat problems
2. **机制句流畅**：用 `deprives ... of` 这类精准动词短语，不堆砌从句
3. **影响句不重复**：`stem from overwork` 而非重复 "caused by working overtime"

### 1. 核心搭配与高阶词汇 (Vocabulary Bank)

我们要学习的是“词组”，而不是孤立的单词。

#### A. 描述现象与危害
* **The widespread practice of...**：……的普遍做法（比 "Many people do..." 高级得多）。
* **Working long hours**：长工时/加班（地道表达）。
* **Pose a serious threat to...**：对……构成严重威胁。
* **Physical and psychological well-being**：身心健康（雅思健康类话题必背）。
* **High-pressure industries**：高压行业。

#### B. 描述原因与逻辑
* **Prolonged overwork**：长期的过度劳累。
* **Deprive individuals of...**：剥夺了某人的……（用法：`deprive sb. of sth.`）。
    * *例句：Sleep deprivation deprives students of concentration.*
* **Adequate rest**：充足的休息。
* **Be essential for...**：对……至关重要。

#### C. 引用证据与结果
* **Studies conducted by... indicate that...**：由……开展的研究表明……（标准的证据引用句式）。
* **Be significantly more likely to...**：显著地更有可能……（描述概率增加）。
* **Chronic stress-related conditions**：长期的压力相关疾病。
* **Mounting pressure**：日益增长的压力。

### 2. 疑难点解析：`stem directly from`

这个短语是描述“因果关系”的高级表达。

* **含义：** 意为“**直接源自于……**”或“**直接由……引起**”。
* **形象理解：** `Stem` 原意是植物的“茎”。就像花朵是从茎上长出来的一样，某个结果（condition/illness）是从某个原因（exhaustion）中“生长”出来的。
    * 
* **用法：** `A (结果) stem from B (原因)`。
* **近义词替换：**
    * *result from* (常用)
    * *be caused by* (基础)
    * *be rooted in* (强调根源)
* **原句拆解：**
    > "...patients whose conditions **stem directly from** work-related exhaustion."
    > （……那些病情**直接源于**工作相关疲劳的患者。）

---

### 3. 仿写练习：把这些词用起来

既然你有 12 年后端经验，我们试着用这些词来写一句关于“技术债务”或“代码维护”的句子：

> **原句意：** 不规范的代码编写习惯对系统的长期稳定性构成了威胁，因为这会导致维护困难。
>
> **套用高阶词：**
> "The **widespread practice of** writing messy code **poses a serious threat to** the long-term stability of software. This is because technical debt **deprives** developers **of** the time needed for innovation. As a result, many systems suffer from bugs that **stem directly from** poor initial design."

---

### 💡 学习建议：

1.  **记忆 `deprive ... of ...`**：这是一个非常有力的词，当你写到“加班让人没时间陪家人”时，可以说：*Overwork **deprives** parents **of** quality time with their children.*
2.  **背诵 `well-being`**：不要只写 "health"，`well-being` 包含了幸福感和健康，层次更高。
