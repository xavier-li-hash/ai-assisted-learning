# 写作 Week 1 - Day 3：让步与对比句训练

> 目标：能写"虽然…但是…"（让步）和"A与B相比…"（对比）的逻辑句
> 重点句型：`Although`, `While`, `However`, `Unlike`, `Compared to`, `whereas`
> 考试关联：Task 2 议论文中让步段落；Task 1 数据对比描述

---

## 今日语法焦点

### 让步句（先承认反面，再说核心观点）

```
Although [承认的反面观点], [你的核心论点].
```
> Although social media can be a distraction, it also enables people to stay informed and connected across long distances.

### 对比句（比较两者差异）

```
Unlike [A], [B] tends to [V].
Compared to [A], [B] is more [adj] because [原因].
[A] ..., whereas [B] ...
```
> Unlike traditional classrooms, online learning allows students to study at their own pace.
> Rural areas tend to have cleaner air, whereas cities often suffer from higher levels of pollution.

**关键区别：**
- `Although` / `While` → 让步（承认一面，转折另一面）
- `Unlike` / `Compared to` / `whereas` → 对比（并列两个不同事物）

## II. Task 1 专项：数据对比描述句训练

> **考试场景：** Task 1 body 段不仅要描述趋势，还要对比两个类别（如男性/女性、国家A/B、不同年份）。
> **核心原则：** 配上具体数字 + 时间 + 对比连接词

**数据对比核心句型：**

| 句型 | 用法 |
|------|------|
| `[A] stood at X%, compared to Y% for [B].` | 并列数字对比 |
| `While [A] accounted for X%, [B] represented only Y%.` | while 对比 |
| `[A] had the highest figure at X%, followed by [B] at Y%.` | 排序描述 |
| `Both A and B changed: the former rose while the latter fell.` | 双项趋势对比 |

> **公式：让步（Although...） → 转折（However...） → 具体例子（For instance...） → 影响（This suggests...）**

---

**句2 — While（让步句）**

| 问题 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| `more inefficient` → `less efficient` | inefficient 已是负面词，加 more 语义重复；反义用 less efficient |

✅ 改正：
> While working from home can offer greater flexibility and reduce commuting time, many employees find that it **leads to less efficient communication** and **undermines their overall productivity**.

**句3 — However（转折）**

> 你写的：`most modern citizens cannot balance work and life in such a rapid competive society, therefore, they don't have adiqute time for sports.`

|------|------|

| `sports` → `regular exercise` | 学术写作中 regular exercise 比 sports 更精准 |

✅ 改正：
> Regular exercise is widely recognised as essential for maintaining good health. However, in today's **fast-paced and competitive** society, many people struggle to find **adequate** time for **regular exercise**.

✅ 改正：
> Unlike **urban areas**, **rural areas** tend to have cleaner air and **lower levels of noise pollution**.

| `guiding students think` | 缺 to，应用不定式 | `guiding students **to** think critically` |

✅ P1 改正版：
> Although online learning offers great flexibility, it can often fail to **provide the hands-on practice** that students need to fully grasp complex concepts. However, when **real-world practice opportunities** are incorporated, learners tend to demonstrate a **deeper understanding** and the ability to apply knowledge across different **scenarios**. For instance, a study found that students who applied theoretical knowledge to real-world **problems** achieved approximately 20% higher scores compared to those who passively watched lectures without engaging with the content. This suggests that the key to effective online learning lies not in **delivering content**, but in **guiding students to think critically** and apply what they have learned.
