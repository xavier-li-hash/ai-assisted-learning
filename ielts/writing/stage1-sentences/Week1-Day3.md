# 写作 Week 1 - Day 3：让步与对比句训练

> 目标：能写"虽然…但是…"（让步）和"A与B相比…"（对比）的逻辑句
> 重点句型：`Although`, `While`, `However`, `Unlike`, `Compared to`, `whereas`
> 考试关联：Task 2 议论文中让步段落；Task 1 数据对比描述

---

## 今日语法焦点

### 让步句（先承认反面，再说核心观点）

```
Although [承认的反面观点], [你的核心论点].
```
> Although social media can be a distraction, it also enables people to stay informed and connected across long distances.

### 对比句（比较两者差异）

```
Unlike [A], [B] tends to [V].
Compared to [A], [B] is more [adj] because [原因].
[A] ..., whereas [B] ...
```
> Unlike traditional classrooms, online learning allows students to study at their own pace.
> Rural areas tend to have cleaner air, whereas cities often suffer from higher levels of pollution.

**关键区别：**
- `Although` / `While` → 让步（承认一面，转折另一面）
- `Unlike` / `Compared to` / `whereas` → 对比（并列两个不同事物）

## II. Task 1 专项：数据对比描述句训练

> **考试场景：** Task 1 body 段不仅要描述趋势，还要对比两个类别（如男性/女性、国家A/B、不同年份）。
> **核心原则：** 配上具体数字 + 时间 + 对比连接词

**数据对比核心句型：**

| 句型 | 用法 |
|------|------|
| `[A] stood at X%, compared to Y% for [B].` | 并列数字对比 |
| `While [A] accounted for X%, [B] represented only Y%.` | while 对比 |
| `[A] had the highest figure at X%, followed by [B] at Y%.` | 排序描述 |
| `Both A and B changed: the former rose while the latter fell.` | 双项趋势对比 |

### 复习 C：语法辨析（共3题）

**RC1.** 以下两句哪个正确？为什么？
```
(a) He cannot control how much he eat.
(b) He cannot control how much he eats.
→
```
<details><summary>答案</summary>

**(b) 正确**。主语是 he（第三人称单数），动词须加 -s → eats

---

**RC2.** `fewer` 和 `less` 分别用于什么情况？各举一例：
```
fewer →
less  →
```
<details><summary>答案</summary>

- **fewer** 用于可数名词：fewer books / fewer cars / fewer students
- **less** 用于不可数名词：less time / less water / less exercise

</details>

---

**RC3.** 以下句子哪里有问题？改正它：
```
原句：The widespread practice of working overtime cause serious health problems.
问题：
改正：
```
<details><summary>答案</summary>

**问题**：主语是 "The widespread practice"（单数），动词应为 **causes** 而非 cause

**改正**：The widespread practice of working overtime **causes** serious health problems.

## IV. 逻辑链条段落练习

> **今日挑战：让步+转折型段落结构**
>
> 这是 Task 2 议论文最常用的段落模式——先承认对方有道理，再说自己的论点更重要。
>
> **公式：让步（Although...） → 转折（However...） → 具体例子（For instance...） → 影响（This suggests...）**

---

**练习 P1（半提示）：**
```
抽象句：Online learning is not always effective.

→ 让步句：Although online learning offers great flexibility,
  it can often fail to ______________________________________.
  （提示：它缺乏什么？师生互动？实践操作？监督机制？）

→ 转折（主要论点）：However, when ______________________________
  are incorporated, learners tend to show ______________________.
  （提示：加入什么要素可以改善？线上答疑/协作实验室？）

→ 具体例子：For instance, a study found that students who
  ______________________________ achieved ____% higher scores
  compared to those who ______________________________________.
  （提示：加入具体数据，大约20%）

→ 影响：This suggests that the key to effective online learning
  lies not in _________________________, but in _________________.
  （提示：不在于内容交付，而在于互动设计）
```

## 7分示例参考（对照自查）

> 写完自己的练习后，再看这里对比差距。

**让步句 7分示例：**

> Although working from home can offer greater flexibility, many employees struggle to maintain clear boundaries between professional and personal life, which can ultimately reduce overall productivity.

**Task 1 对比句 7分示例（对应 T-1）：**

> Men worked an average of 48 hours per week in 2020, compared to 38 hours for women, suggesting a notable gap in working hours between genders.

**逻辑链段落 7分示例（对应 P2）：**

> Admittedly, living in the countryside often means having limited access to specialised employment and advanced medical facilities. However, these disadvantages are frequently outweighed by significant benefits, including a substantially lower cost of living and a healthier environment that many urban residents increasingly value. For example, housing costs in rural areas can be 30–40% lower than in comparable cities, while residents also benefit from cleaner air and quieter surroundings that reduce chronic stress. Therefore, while the countryside may lack the dynamism of urban centres, it offers a quality of life that many people find more sustainable and fulfilling in the long term.

**逐句分析（7分与6分的差距在哪里）：**

| 段落层 | 6分写法 | 7分写法 | 差距点 |
|--------|---------|---------|--------|
| 让步 | Although countryside has problems, | Admittedly, ... often means having **limited access to** | 动词搭配更精准 |
| 转折 | But it also has benefits | these disadvantages are **frequently outweighed by** | outweigh 是高分词 |
| 例子 | houses are cheaper | housing costs can be **30–40% lower** | 数字+比较结构 |
| 结论 | So countryside is good | a quality of life **more sustainable and fulfilling** | 形容词组合更有力 |
