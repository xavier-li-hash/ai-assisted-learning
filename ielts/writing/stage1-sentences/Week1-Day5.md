## Task 1 专项：流程图 & 地图描述句训练

> **考试场景：** Task 1 偶尔出现流程图（process diagram）和地图（map），描述方式与数据图完全不同——不谈变化趋势，而是描述步骤顺序或地理位置变化。

---

### 流程图描述句型

**核心逻辑：** 用顺序连接词（First → Then → Next → After that → Finally）描述每个步骤，动词用被动语态为主。

```
First, [raw material] is [processed/treated/transported] to [location/stage].
After this, [the product] is [verb-ed] in order to [purpose].
Finally, [the finished product] is [distributed/packaged/stored].
```

**P-1.** 用被动语态描述流程步骤：
```
流程：原材料 → 被运送到工厂 → 被清洗 → 被切割 → 被包装 → 发送给消费者
写出前3个步骤的句子：
First, the raw materials ______.
They are then ______ before being ______.
```

**P-2.** 连接两个步骤，用 `Once... it is then...` 句型：
```
流程步骤：水先被过滤，过滤完后才加热。
你的句子：Once the water ______, it is then ______.
```

---

### 地图描述句型

**核心逻辑：** 描述地点变化（新增/拆除/移动）+ 位置关系（方位词）。不写趋势，写位置和变化。

**位置描述词汇：**
```
to the north/south/east/west of
in the centre/on the outskirts of
adjacent to / next to / opposite
where [X] once stood, there is now [Y]
```

**变化描述句型：**
```
The [building/road/area] has been replaced by ______.
A new [facility] has been constructed [to the east of / next to] ______.
What was formerly [X] is now [Y].
The [area] to the north of the river has been developed into ______.
```

**M-1.** 描述一处"新增"的变化：
```
地图信息：2020年，学校东边原来是空地，现在建了一个停车场。
你的句子：To the east of the school, ______ has been replaced by ______.
或：A ______ has been constructed ______.
```

**M-2.** 用 `What was formerly... is now...` 描述用途改变：
```
地图信息：原来的工厂区现在变成了住宅区。
你的句子：What was formerly ______ is now ______.
```

**M-3.** 综合写2句话描述下面的地图变化：

### 复习 A 答案

**RA1.** **more likely to** get chronic diseases
（probable 通常不直接接 to + 动词；likely to 是固定搭配，这是本周出现3次的高频错误）

**RA2.** **Car sales peaked in 2015, whereas electric vehicle sales rose steadily.**
（whereas 是从属连词，必须与主句在同一句中；不能用句号断开后独立开头）

**RA3.** **(b) 正确**
（不能用 Who 直接开头作主语，必须在前面加 Those / People：**Those who** work overtime...）

**RA4.**
- (1) **deep**（修饰名词 understanding，用形容词）
- (2) **deeply**（修饰动词 understands，用副词）

**RA5.** **men's working hours** 或 **the working hours of men**
（"working time" 不地道；"work time of male" 双重错误）

**RA6.** **Factories generate constant noise pollution in cities.**

### 复习 C 答案

**RC1.**
错误点：
1. By → **With / Due to**（by 表方式，不表原因）
2. greenhouse effect → **the greenhouse effect**（缺定冠词）
3. tend → **tends**（单数主语）
4. increasely → **increasingly**

改正句：**Due to the rapid development of modern life, the greenhouse effect tends to become an increasingly serious problem.**

**RC2.**
错误点：
1. smartphone → **smartphones**（复数）
2. over the world → **around / across the world**
3. negative effect → **negative effects**（复数）
4. across children → **among children**（across 表地理范围，among 表人群）
5. which led to → **which leads to**（时态应与前文一致）
6. accompany with their fiends → **spending time with their friends**（词性错 + 拼写错 fiends→friends）

改正句：**The use of smartphones has significantly increased around the world, causing negative effects among children and adults, which leads to less time for reading and spending time with their friends and families.**
