## 今日语法焦点：两种让步句的区别

Week 1 你用过 although / even though / while。今天加入一个更学术的让步结构：

```
❶ Although / Even though + 完整从句, 主句.
   Although fast food is affordable, it poses serious health risks.

❷ Despite / In spite of + 名词/动名词短语, 主句.
   Despite its affordability, fast food poses serious health risks.
   In spite of being convenient, fast food is nutritionally poor.
```

**区别记忆**：
- Although/even though 后跟**句子**（有主语+动词）
- Despite/in spite of 后跟**名词短语**（没有主语动词）

## Task 1 专项：饼图描述句训练

> **考试场景：** 饼图描述与折线图不同——没有趋势，只有比例和排名。核心是比较各类别的比重。

---

### 饼图核心句型

```
[Category] accounted for the largest share, at X%.
[Category] made up nearly half of the total, at X%.
[A] and [B] together represented X% of [total].
The proportion of [category] was X%, slightly higher than [B] at Y%.
[The smallest share] belonged to [category], which stood at only X%.
```

## Day 5 复习答案汇总

> 做完复习部分后再看。

### 复习 A 答案

**RA1.** **office workers** 或 **white-collar workers**
（officers = 军官/警官/高级官员；描述普通上班族用 office workers）

**RA2.** heat → **heated** → **heated**（规则动词；❌ hotted 不存在）

**RA3.** cut → **cut** → **cut**（不规则动词，三态相同；❌ cutted 不存在）

**RA4.** **a residential area** / **a residential zone**
（residential 是形容词；resident 是名词，不能直接修饰 zone）

**RA5.** **increasingly serious**（increasingly 是副词，修饰形容词 serious；increasing 是形容词/分词，不能修饰另一个形容词）

---

### 复习 B 答案

**RB1.** those who **use** AI tools
（关系从句中，who 后接动词原形，不接 -ing；"those who using" 是中式英语直译错误）

**RB2.** **take action**（固定短语，无冠词；✅ take action ❌ take an action ❌ take actions）

**RB3.**
- 错误1：`more probable to` → `more **likely** to`（固定搭配）
- 错误2：`themself` → `**themselves**`（反身代词复数）
- 改正：The residents are more **likely** to relax after moving to rural areas.

---

### 复习 C 答案

**RC1.**
- 错误1：`By` → **Due to / With**（by 表方式，不表原因；due to 表因果）
- 错误2：`greenhouse effect` → **the greenhouse effect**（特指，需定冠词）
- 错误3：`tend` → **tends**（单数主语 greenhouse effect）
- 错误4：`increasely` → **increasingly**（-ly 副词形式；注意没有 increasely 这个词）

改正句：**Due to the rapid development of modern life, the greenhouse effect tends to become an increasingly serious problem.**

### 今日 5 句批改

**句1（目的句）**
> In order to alleviate traffic congestion, the gonverment should reform traffic regulations and invest in infrastucture upgrades.

✅ `alleviate`、`the government`、结构全部正确——这是 Day 5 错过的两处，今天修好了。
❌ **gonverment** → **government**（拼写）
❌ **infrastucture** → **infrastructure**（拼写，注意 infra-**struc**-ture）

> 7分版：In order to alleviate traffic congestion, **the government** should reform traffic regulations and invest in **infrastructure** upgrades.

---

**句2（因果句）**
> The time of using mobilephones has significantly increasing, which leads to serious effects to teenagers such as less frequentity of reading and fewer physical activities.

❌ "The time of using mobilephones" → **Mobile phone use** / **The amount of time spent on mobile phones**（更地道的名词化）
❌ "has significantly **increasing**" → **has significantly increased**（现在完成时：has + **过去分词**，不是 -ing）
❌ "effects **to**" → **effects on**（固定搭配：have an effect **on**，不是 to）
❌ "**frequentity**" → 这个词不存在。频率 = **frequency**；或直接用 "less frequent reading"（形容词）

青少年 = **teenagers / adolescents / young people**

关于 `such as` 你的问题：**such as 后面跟名词/名词短语**
- ✅ such as **reading less frequently** and **exercising less**（动名词短语）
- ✅ such as **a decline in reading** and **fewer physical activities**（名词短语）
- ❌ such as they read less（不能跟完整句子）

> 7分版：**Mobile phone use** has significantly **increased**, which leads to serious **effects on** teenagers, such as a decline in **reading frequency** and fewer physical activities.

---

**句3（让步句 — Despite / In spite of）**
> In spite of its convenience, traditional classes have more interactivities and realtime feedbacks.

✅ `In spite of + 名词短语` 结构正确。
⚠️ "its convenience" 指代不清——"its" 是谁的？应明确：**In spite of the convenience of online learning**, traditional classes...
❌ "**interactivities**" → **more interaction** / **are more interactive**（interaction 是名词，不加 -ities）
❌ "**realtime feedbacks**" → **real-time feedback**（real-time 需连字符；feedback 是不可数名词，不加 s）

> 7分版：**In spite of the convenience of online learning**, traditional classes offer **more interaction** and enable students to receive **real-time feedback** from teachers.

---

**句4（对比句）**
> Compared to the noise pollution, living in countryside is more likely to be sustainable and fulfilling.

❌ **逻辑错误**：`Compared to noise pollution` vs `living in countryside` — 这两个不是同类事物，不能比较。对比句两端必须是同类型的东西。
应改为：**Compared to city life** / **Compared to urban areas**, living in the countryside...
❌ "in **countryside**" → "in **the** countryside"（需定冠词）
❌ "**more likely to** be sustainable" → `more likely to` 表示可能性，此处应用 **tends to be** / **is generally more** sustainable

> 7分版：**Compared to city life**, living in **the** countryside **tends to be** more sustainable and fulfilling, as residents face less noise pollution and a slower pace of daily life.

---

**句5（自选/具体化）**
> Since intelligence artifitial is advancing rapidly, those who work in office using computers should use AI tools to enhance their efficiency to reach higher achievement.

❌ "**intelligence artifitial**" → **artificial intelligence**（形容词在前：artificial + intelligence；同时拼写错误）
❌ "work in **office**" → "work **in an office**" 或直接写 "**office workers**"
❌ "using computers" → 冗余，删掉
❌ "to **reach higher achievement**" → **to achieve greater productivity**（reach achievement 搭配生硬）
✅ `those who work` — `those who + 动词原形` 本次用对了！

> 7分版：**Since artificial intelligence** is advancing rapidly, **office workers** who adopt AI tools to enhance their efficiency are more likely to **achieve greater productivity** and outperform their peers.
