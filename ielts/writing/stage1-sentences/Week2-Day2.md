## 今日语法焦点：结果状语 — 3种写法

Week 1 你用过 `which leads to` 和 `leading to`。今天补充第3种更学术的写法：

```
写法 1：...which leads to + 名词
  → Mobile phone overuse leads to sleep disorders.

写法 2：...leading to + 名词（分词短语）
  → Mobile phones are overused, leading to sleep disorders.

写法 3：...thereby + V-ing（更学术）
  → Mobile phones are overused, thereby causing disruption to normal sleep patterns.
  → Governments should invest in public transport, thereby reducing traffic congestion.
```

**thereby** 相当于 "from which / as a result"，后接动名词，常用于正式写作。

## Task 1 专项：表格描述句训练

> **考试场景：** 表格（table）是雅思 Task 1 中最常见的题型之一。与饼图不同，表格可以同时显示多个类别和多个时间点，需要选择最显著的数据来写。

---

### 表格描述策略

```
步骤 1：Overview 先写最大特征（哪类最高/最低？总体趋势是涨是跌？）
步骤 2：Body 按类别或按时间段分组，每句写1–2个数据
步骤 3：不要把所有数字都写进去，选3–4个最显著的
```

**表格专用句型：**
```
[Country/Category] recorded the highest figure, at X units in [year].
The figures for [A] and [B] were broadly similar, at X and Y respectively.
[Category] saw a notable increase from X in [year 1] to Y in [year 2].
By contrast, [Category] experienced a sharp decline, falling from X to Y.
[Category] remained relatively stable throughout the period, at around X.
```

---

### 今日练习（5句）

> 参考下面的模拟表格：

**表格：三个国家的人均咖啡消耗量（杯/年）**

| 国家 | 2000 | 2010 | 2020 |
|------|------|------|------|
| Finland | 420 | 450 | 480 |
| Brazil | 180 | 220 | 310 |
| China | 15 | 40 | 120 |

---

**T-1.** 用 `recorded the highest figure` 描述某国某年最高值：
```
Finland recorded the highest figure, at 480 in 2020.
```

**T-2.** 用 `saw a notable increase from X to Y` 描述变化最大的国家：
```
China saw a notable increase from 15 in 2000 to 120 in 2020.
```

**T-3.** 用 `remained relatively stable` 描述变化最小的（注意：Finland 是稳定增长还是稳定不变？改用合适句型）：
```
提示：Finland 是缓慢增长，所以不用 remained stable，用 grew steadily from X to Y。
写出 Finland 的趋势句：

Finland grew steadily from 420 in 2000 to 480 in 2020.

```

**T-4.** 用 `By contrast` 对比两国2020年数据：
```
By contrast, China experienced a sharp rise, increasing from 40 to 120. While Finland just grew 30.
```

**T-5.** 写一句 Overview（用 Overall 开头，概括最大特征，不写具体数字）：
```
Overall, it is clear that Finland remained highest level through the period, whereas China represented the smallest figure, but experienced a sharp rise. Further more, Brazil grew steadily and laid in the middle.
```

**自查：**
- [ ] 写数字时单位是否一致（杯/年，不要写成%）
- [ ] Overview 不写具体数字，只写趋势/特征
- [ ] "remained stable" 只用于真的没有变化的数据
