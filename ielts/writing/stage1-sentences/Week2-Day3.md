### 今日 7 句批改

---

**句1（目的句）**
> In order to alleviate the effect of energy exhausting, the government tries to spread the use of renewable energy, such as wind energy, sarly energy.

❌ "**energy exhausting**" → **the depletion of fossil fuels** / **excessive reliance on fossil fuels**（energy exhausting 不是固定说法）
❌ "**sarly energy**" → **solar energy**（太阳能；solar = 太阳的）
❌ "**tries to spread the use of**" → **promotes the use of** / **promotes the adoption of**（更地道）

> ✅ 7分版：In order to reduce reliance on fossil fuels, the government **promotes** the use of renewable energy, such as wind energy and **solar** energy.

---

**句2（因果句 + 不可数名词）**
> Educators have strongly pubulished their advice for asking decreasing the use of mobile phone, since teenagers have used socicial medias excessively, which affects their study progress and takes a damage on their physical and mental health.

❌ "**pubulished their advice**" → **offered advice** / **urged** （publish 是出版/发布文章，不用于建议）
❌ "**advice for asking decreasing**" → **advice on reducing**（固定搭配：advice **on** + V-ing）
❌ "**socicial medias**" → **social media**（social media 是不可数名词，不加 s；social 拼写）
❌ "**study progress**" → **academic performance**（学业表现）
❌ "**takes a damage on**" → **has a negative impact on** / **harms**（damage 作名词：causes damage to；这里直接用 harm/damage 作动词）
✅ `advice`（不可数）没有加 s ——注意到了！
✅ `physical and mental health` ✅

> ✅ 7分版：**Since** teenagers have been using social media excessively, educators have **offered advice on reducing** mobile phone use, warning of its harmful **effects on** academic performance and physical and mental health.

---

**句3（让步句 — Despite）**
> Despite the negative effect of fast food, it is still welcome to most citizens as its convenience and affordablity, solving the daily meal requirement for each employees who are working in urban area and have no time to cook by themselves.

❌ "**welcome to**" → **popular among**（"welcome to" = 欢迎来到某地；"受欢迎" = **popular among**）
❌ "**as its**" → **due to its**（原因用 due to，不用 as its）
❌ "**affordablity**" → **affordability**（afford-**ability**）
❌ "**each employees**" → **employees**（each 后跟单数；或直接用 employees）
❌ "**cook by themselves**" → **cook for themselves**（自己做饭 = cook **for** themselves）

> ✅ 7分版：Despite its well-documented health risks, fast food remains **popular among** most city residents **due to** its convenience and **affordability**, as it provides a quick meal solution for employees who have no time to cook **for** themselves.

---

**句4（对比句 — while / whereas）**
> While the access of education resources in developed countries is satisfied since decades ago, whereas it is still not meet the least requirement in developing countries.

❌ **双重连词**：`While...whereas` 只能用一个，不能连用
❌ "**access of**" → **access to**（固定搭配：access **to** resources）
❌ "**is satisfied**" → **is sufficient** / **has been well-established**
❌ "**since decades ago**" → **for decades**（for + 时间段；since + 时间点）
❌ "**is still not meet**" → **still does not meet**（助动词后用动词原形）
❌ "**the least requirement**" → **basic educational standards** / **the minimum requirements**

> ✅ 7分版：While access to educational resources in developed countries **has been well-established for decades**, it **still does not meet basic standards** in many developing countries.

---

**句5（thereby + V-ing）**
> Companies adopt energy saving technologies, thereby reducing the requirement of energy and the cost of the production.

✅ `thereby + V-ing` 结构完全正确！本周新句型已掌握。
❌ "**the requirement of energy**" → **energy consumption** / **the demand for energy**
❌ "**the cost of the production**" → **production costs**（更简洁地道）

> ✅ 7分版：Companies adopt **energy-saving** technologies, thereby reducing **energy consumption** and **production costs**.
（这句整体很好，只需替换两处词汇即可到7分）

---

**句6（自选 + 现在完成时）**
> Despite less time spended on commuting, work from home has a negative effect on communication and co-operation among employees, leading to lower efficiency of productivity.

❌ "**spended**" → **spent**（spend → **spent** → **spent**，不规则动词）
❌ 缺少现在完成时：**has had** a negative effect（题目要求 has/have + 过去分词）
❌ "**efficiency of productivity**" → **overall productivity** / **work efficiency**

关于你的问题：
- **写作（名词）** = writing；**一篇文章** = an essay / a piece of writing
- **大型企业** = large corporations / major companies；corporation 更正式，company 更通用
- **学院** = college（两年制/专科）/ university（综合大学）；雅思写作通常用 university
- **同事** = colleagues（正式）/ co-workers（口语）/ peers（同级别的人）

> ✅ 7分版：Despite the reduction in commuting time, working from home **has had** a negative effect on communication and co-operation among employees, leading to lower **overall productivity**.

---

**句7（自选 + 具体数据/地点）**
> Citinasation has signfincant developed in sothern of China in recent decades, which leads to deterioration on environmental system, such as the damage on forest causing inbalance between animals and ecosystem.

❌ "**Citinasation**" → **Urbanisation**（城市化；urban = 城市的；-isation = 化）
❌ "**signfincant developed**" → **significantly developed** / **advanced significantly**（副词 significantly 修饰动词）
❌ "**sothern of China**" → **southern China**（southern + 地名，不加 of）
❌ "**deterioration on**" → **deterioration of / in**（deterioration of the environment）
❌ "**environmental system**" → **the environment** / **ecosystems**
❌ "**inbalance**" → **imbalance**（前缀是 im-，不是 in-）

> ✅ 7分版：**Urbanisation** has advanced significantly in **southern China** in recent decades, leading to serious **deterioration of the environment**, including widespread deforestation that has disrupted the **balance** between wildlife and **ecosystems**.

## 今日语法焦点：条件句 — 3种写法

```
写法 1（真实条件）：If + 一般现在时, 主句用 will / should / can
  → If governments invest in public transport, traffic congestion will decrease.
  → If companies adopt renewable energy, they can reduce their carbon footprint.

写法 2（否定条件 — unless）：Unless + 肯定句 = if + 否定句
  → Unless governments take action, air pollution will worsen.
  （= If governments do not take action...）

写法 3（更正式 — provided that）：Provided that + 从句
  → Provided that adequate funding is secured, the project can be completed on time.
  → Provided that students have access to the internet, online learning can be effective.
```

**关键点：**
- `If` 条件句中，从句用**一般现在时**（不用 will）：✅ If it **rains** / ❌ If it **will rain**
- `Unless` 后面跟**肯定句**，因为 unless 本身含否定
- `provided that` 比 if 更正式，常用于学术写作

## Task 1 专项：线图（Line Graph）描述训练

> **考试场景：** 线图是雅思 Task 1 最高频题型，重点是描述**变化趋势**和**转折点**。

---

### 线图核心句型

```
趋势句：
[Category] rose sharply from X to Y between [year1] and [year2].
[Category] declined steadily from X to Y over the same period.
[Category] peaked at X in [year], before falling to Y by [year2].
[Category] remained relatively stable at around X throughout the period.

转折句：
After rising to X in [year], [category] then fell sharply to Y by [year2].
Having peaked at X in [year], [category] experienced a gradual decline.

对比两线：
While [A] increased significantly, [B] showed a steady decline over the same period.
[A] and [B] followed opposite trends throughout the period.
```
