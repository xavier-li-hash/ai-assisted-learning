### 今日 7 句批改

---

**句1（条件句 if）**
> If government invest more in education, the quality of nation will improve.

❌ "**government**" → **governments** 或 **the government**（漏 s 或 the）
❌ "**the quality of nation**" → **citizens' quality of life** / **educational standards**（nation 不能直接表示国民素质）
✅ if 从句用一般现在时 invest ——已正确！

> ✅ 7分版：If governments invest more in education, citizens' quality of life will improve significantly.

---

**句2（条件句 unless）**
> Unless take an international level action, the greenhouse effect will develop more serious.

❌ **unless 从句缺主语**：Unless **the international community** takes action（从句必须有主语+谓语）
❌ "**an international level action**" → **international action** / **collective international action**
❌ "**will develop more serious**" → **will become increasingly serious** / **will worsen**（develop 不能这样搭配）

> ✅ 7分版：Unless the international community takes collective action, the greenhouse effect will become increasingly serious.

---

**句3（provided that）**
> Provided that employees work from home, the overall productivity will decrease due to higher cost of communication and co-operation.

❌ **语义错误**：provided that 引导"正面可实现的前提条件"，不适合描述负面结果。这里逻辑是"只要员工在家工作→生产力下降"，不是 provided that 的典型用法。
❌ "**higher cost**" → **increased costs** / **higher costs**（成本用复数 costs 更自然）

> ✅ 7分版：Provided that employees have access to reliable internet connections and communication tools, working from home can maintain overall productivity levels.

---

**句4（Despite + if 组合）**
> Despite excessive urbanization has some nagetive effects...

❌ **Despite 后不能跟完整句子**！Despite + 名词短语：**Despite its negative effects**（尽管有其负面影响）
❌ "**nagetive**" → **negative**
❌ "**such modern productivity**" → **such as modern** productivity（举例用 such as）
❌ "**outweight**" → **outweigh**（拼写）
❌ "**the government invest**" → **the government invests**（第三人称单数）

另外：题目要求写成一句，你写了两句——可以用分号或连词合并。

> ✅ 7分版：Despite its negative effects on the environment, urbanisation **outweighs** its drawbacks in terms of economic growth and productivity; if governments invest more in city planning, problems such as ecosystem imbalance can be effectively addressed.

---

**句5（因果句 + 不可数名词）**
> Teengers have been using social media execesively in recent years, which leads to nagetive impact on academic performance and physical and mental health.

❌ "**Teengers**" → **Teenagers**（拼写）
❌ "**execesively**" → **excessively**（拼写：exces-sive-ly）
❌ "**leads to nagetive impact**" → **has had a negative impact on**（leads to + 名词 需要冠词：leads to **a** negative impact；且与前面完成时配合用 has had 更一致）
✅ social media 没有加 s ——很好！

> ✅ 7分版：Teenagers have been using social media excessively in recent years, which **has had** a **negative** impact on their academic performance and physical and mental health.

---

**句6（对比句 while）**
> While developed countries have reformed more laws to protect environment, alleviating different pollution problems, including less emition of carban diocide, most developing countries are still trying to sacrify environment to promote/boost their economy development.

❌ "**reformed more laws**" → **enacted stricter environmental laws** / **introduced stronger environmental regulations**
❌ "**protect environment**" → **protect **the** environment**（需要冠词）
❌ "**emition**" → **emission**（拼写：e-mis-sion）
❌ "**carban diocide**" → **carbon dioxide**（碳 carbon；二氧化物 dioxide）
❌ "**sacrify**" → **sacrifice**（拼写）
❌ "**economy development**" → **economic development**（economic 是形容词，修饰名词）
❌ **未使用** access to 和 for decades（题目要求）

> ✅ 7分版：While developed countries have had **access to** stricter environmental regulations **for decades**, reducing **carbon dioxide emissions** significantly, most developing countries still **sacrifice** the environment to pursue **economic** development.

---

**句7（自选 + 现在完成时）**
> Artificial intelligence has been adoptted in medical area, thereby improving efficiency of treatment to every patient.

❌ "**adoptted**" → **adopted**（adopt → adopted，只有一个 t）
❌ "**medical area**" → **the medical field** / **healthcare**
❌ "**efficiency of treatment to**" → **the efficiency of treatment **for** patients**（treatment 用 for，且 efficiency 需要冠词 the）
❌ 缺少**具体人群或数据**（题目要求）

- 动词：**diagnose**（诊断某人：diagnose a patient with cancer）

## 今日语法焦点：关系从句（定语从句）

```
类型 1 — 限制性定语从句（不加逗号）
修饰先行词，缺少它句意不完整

  who   → 指人
  The students who study abroad often develop greater independence.

  which / that → 指物
  The policy that the government introduced has reduced unemployment.

类型 2 — 非限制性定语从句（加逗号）
补充说明先行词，去掉也不影响主句意思；只能用 which（不用 that）

  Online learning, which has grown rapidly in recent years, offers
  greater flexibility for working adults.

  The government introduced a new tax policy, which led to a
  significant increase in public revenue.

类型 3 — where / whose
  where → 指地点
  Urban areas where pollution levels are high often have higher
  rates of respiratory disease.

  whose → 指所属
  Children whose parents have higher education tend to perform
  better academically.
```

**关键规则：**
- 非限制性从句必须用 **which**，不能用 that
- 非限制性从句前必须加**逗号**
- which 引导非限制性从句时，可以指代前面**整个句子的内容**（最常用！）
