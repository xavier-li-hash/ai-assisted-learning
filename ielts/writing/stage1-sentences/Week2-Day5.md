## 语法焦点：让步句三种句型

```
类型 1 — Although（尽管……但是……）
后接完整从句（主语 + 谓语）

  Although online learning is convenient, it lacks face-to-face interaction.
  Although renewable energy is expanding, fossil fuels still dominate the market.

类型 2 — Despite（尽管……）
后接名词短语（不能跟完整句子！）

  ✅ Despite its high cost, solar energy is becoming increasingly popular.
  ✅ Despite rapid economic growth, inequality remains a serious issue.
  ❌ Despite it is expensive → 错！（it is expensive 是完整句子）

类型 3 — While（一方面……另一方面……）
强调对比，两边都有完整句子

  While technology has improved productivity, it has also caused job displacement.
  While developed countries benefit from globalisation, developing nations often struggle.
```

**关键规则：**
- Despite / In spite of → 后接**名词 / 名词短语 / V-ing**
- Although / Even though → 后接**完整从句**
- While → 后接**完整从句**（用于对比）

## 比较结构补充

```
基本对比：
  A is more + adj + than B
  A is less + adj + than B
  A is as + adj + as B
  A is not as + adj + as B

搭配副词强调（雅思加分）：
  A is significantly more expensive than B.
  A is considerably more effective than B.
  A is far less reliable than B.

倍数表达（复习）：
  A is twice as high as B.   （2倍）
  A is three times as high as B.   （3倍）
  A is four times as large as B.   （4倍）
  ✅ 记忆：只有"2倍"用 twice，3倍起统一用 X times as ... as
```
