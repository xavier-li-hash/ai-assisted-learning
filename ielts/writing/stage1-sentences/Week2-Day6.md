## Task 1 专项：柱状图（Bar Chart）描述训练

> **考试场景：** 柱状图重点描述各类别在不同时间点或不同组别的数值对比，常用趋势词 + 具体数据。

---

### 柱状图核心句型

```
描述最高/最低值：
[Category] recorded the highest figure at X%, while [B] had the lowest at Y%.
[A] significantly outperformed [B], reaching X% compared to Y%.

描述变化趋势（时间轴柱状图）：
The figure for [A] increased from X% in [year] to Y% in [year].
[A] saw a notable rise/decline between [year] and [year].

并排对比（两组柱）：
[A] consistently exceeded [B] across all categories.
While [A] remained relatively stable, [B] experienced a sharp increase.

Overview 模板：
Overall, [dominant category] recorded the highest figures throughout,
while [lowest category] consistently accounted for the smallest share.
```

---

| 国家 | 2010 | 2020 |
|------|------|------|
| 美国 USA | 72% | 91% |

```

```

## 语法焦点：Week 2 五大句型总结

```
1. 被动语态
   has been + 过去分词（完成时）/ is + 过去分词（一般时）
   ✅ AI has been widely adopted in the medical field.

2. 主谓一致
   找到主语核心词（忽略修饰性从句）→ 判断单复数
   ✅ Urban areas where air quality is poor ARE more likely to have...

3. 定语从句
   who（指人）/ which（指物/整句）/ where（指地点）/ whose（所属）
   非限制性从句（补充信息）→ 前加逗号
   ✅ ...renewable energy, which helps reduce emissions.

4. 让步句
   Although/While + 完整从句；Despite + 名词短语
   ✅ Although online learning has limitations, it can still be convenient.
   ✅ Despite the high cost of housing, young people remain determined to buy.

5. 比较结构
   more...than / as...as / not as...as
   强调副词：significantly / considerably / far
   ✅ Public transport is significantly more affordable than private cars.
```

## Day 5 复习答案汇总

> 做完复习部分后再看。

### 复习 A 答案

**RA1.** ❌ "Despite **it is expensive**" → 改为 **"Despite its high cost"** 或 **"Despite being expensive"**
（Despite 后不能跟完整句子，用名词短语或 V-ing）

**RA2.** "The government **can still be** more proactive..."
（副词 still 放在情态动词 can 之后）

**RA3.** "The rapid growth of **urban areas** has led to a shortage of affordable **housing**."
（urban areas 复数；housing 不可数，无需冠词）

---

### 复习 B 答案（拼写）

**RB1.** Artificial **intelligence**（intel-li-**gence**）
**RB2.** **sacrifice**（sac-ri-**fice**；结尾 -fice 不是 -fic）
**RB3.** **expenditure**（ex-pen-di-**ture**；结尾 -ture）
**RB4.** **transport / transportation**（trans-**port**；不是 transportion）

### 记录希望掌握的单词或知识点

- **副词位置**：情态动词之后，实义动词之前（can **still** be / will **also** cause）
- **Despite** + 名词短语 / V-ing（不能跟完整句子）
- **Overview**：只写最显著特征，不写原因推断，不写具体数字
- 名词数：**emissions / fossil fuels / urban areas / regulations**
- 词性：**valuable**（形容词）vs value（名词/动词）
- 拼写：**sacrifice / expenditure / transportation / intelligence**
