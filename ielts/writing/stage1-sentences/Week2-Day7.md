## Task 1 专项：折线图（Line Graph）描述训练

> **考试场景：** 折线图重点描述**趋势变化**——上升、下降、波动、保持稳定，以及不同线条间的对比。

---

### 折线图核心句型

```
描述上升趋势：
[A] rose steadily from X% in [year] to Y% in [year].
[A] experienced a significant increase, reaching Y% by [year].
There was a sharp rise in [A] between [year1] and [year2].

描述下降趋势：
[A] fell sharply from X to Y between [year1] and [year2].
[A] declined gradually, dropping to Y% by [year].
There was a steady decline in [A] over the period.

描述波动 / 稳定：
[A] fluctuated between X% and Y% throughout the period.
[A] remained relatively stable at around X% for most of the period.

交叉对比：
[A] overtook [B] in [year], reaching X% compared to Y%.
[A] and [B] followed similar trends, though [A] remained consistently higher.

Overview 模板（折线图）：
Overall, [A] showed a consistent upward trend, while [B] experienced a general decline.
Both [A] and [B] ended the period higher than they started.
```

## 语法焦点：Week 2 五大句型终极速查

```
1. 被动语态
   一般时：  is/are + PP     → AI is widely used in hospitals.
   完成时：  has/have been + PP → Renewable energy has been adopted globally.
   进行时：  is being + PP   → The policy is being reviewed.

2. 主谓一致
   核心主语 → 忽略中间修饰语
   ✅ Workers who use AI tools ARE more productive.
   ✅ Working from home HAS increased.

3. 定语从句
   who（人）/ which（物/句）/ where（地点）/ whose（所属）
   非限制性→前加逗号  ✅ ...AI, which has transformed healthcare, is expanding.

4. 让步句
   Although/While + 完整从句
   Despite + 名词短语 / V-ing
   ✅ Although AI is efficient, human oversight remains essential.
   ✅ Despite its high cost, solar energy is growing rapidly.

5. 比较结构
   significantly more/less + adj + than
   as + adj + as / not as + adj + as
   ✅ Urban hospitals are considerably better equipped than rural ones.
```

## Day 6 复习答案汇总

> 做完复习部分后再看。

### 复习 A 答案

**RA1.** "**Working** from home **has** become increasingly common since 2020."
（动名词主语用单数谓语；动名词做主语时写 Working，不写 Work）

**RA2.** "While private **vehicles** are more convenient, public transport is **regarded** as more eco-friendly."
（vehicles 复数；regarded 完整被动态 is regarded）

**RA3.** "Students who live in rural areas **are** far less likely to have access to **high-quality** educational resources than their urban counterparts."
（先行词 students 复数 → are；quality 是名词，形容词用 high-quality）

**RA4.** "Although ageing population poses challenges, elderly workers who **have accumulated** decades of experience can still contribute significantly to the **workforce**."
（accumulate 用现在完成时 have accumulated；workforce = 劳动力）

**RA5.** "Although automation **has displaced** a number of jobs, it has also created new roles **that/which** require advanced technical skills, which may ultimately benefit the broader economy."
（displace 用现在完成时；roles 后用 that/which 引导限制性定语从句）
