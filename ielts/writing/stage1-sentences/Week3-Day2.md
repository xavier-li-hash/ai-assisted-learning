### 本次高频错误

```
① 动词搭配错误（句1）
   ❌ which makes a negative effect on environment
   ✅ which has a negative effect on the environment
   → "effect" 搭配 have，不搭配 make；environment 前需加 the

② Due to 后接动词原形（句3）——高频考点
   ❌ Due to adopt artificial intelligence...
   ✅ Due to the adoption of artificial intelligence...
   → Due to 后必须接名词短语或 V-ing，不能接动词原形

③ 动词搭配：anxious for vs about（句7）
   ❌ anxious for losing their jobs
   ✅ anxious about losing their jobs
   → anxious about（担心某事），anxious for（渴望某事）

④ 冗余表达（句5）
   ❌ return back to work       ✅ return to work
   → return 本身含"回"的意思，back 冗余

⑤ 流程图 P-3 步骤混淆
   ❌ After pulp has been finished...
   ✅ After pulp has been formed...
   → Step 3 是"制浆（form pulp）"，不是"完成"

⑥ 高频拼写错误
   lossing    → losing（一个 s）
   found      → fund（pension fund 养老基金）
   inbalance  → imbalanced（形容词；im- 前缀）
   cizitens   → citizens
   recylical  → linear（流程图是线性的，不是循环的）
```

**Task 2 题目：**
> *Some people think that governments should invest more in public transport. Others believe that money should be spent on building more roads for private vehicles. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.*

**今日任务：写主体段 1——支持发展公共交通的观点**

按4步来，每步写在对应的框里，最后合并成完整段落。

---

**第①步 论点句**——直接陈述本段核心论点，不写 "I think"

```
Investing more in public transport can help to reduce carbon emissions.
```

**第②步 解释句**——用 `This is because...` 或 `This means that...` 说明原因

```
This is because when public transport become more access to most citizens, they prefer to choose public transport rather than drive by themselves.
```

**第③步 例子句**——用 `For example,` 或 `For instance,` 举一个具体例子

```
For example, in Singapore, the government have constructed a merely public transport system, almost 70% of citizens commuting by buses or railway.
```

```
Investing more in public transport can help to reduce carbon emissions. For example, in Singapore, the government have constructed a merely public transport system, almost 70% of citizens commut by buses and railway everyday, which help them save considerable expenditure on holding a private vehicle as well. As a result the traffic congestion is alleviated, and the air qulity is cleaner.
```

## Task 1 专项：饼图 Overview + 数据句

> **Overview 是 Task 1 最关键的失分点**，很多人漏写或写成数据句。
> 规则：Overview **不写具体数字**，只说最主要的趋势和变化。

**数据参考：某城市 2000 年与 2020 年能源来源分布**

| 能源类型 | 2000年 | 2020年 |
|---------|-------|-------|
| 煤炭 Coal | 55% | 30% |
| 天然气 Natural Gas | 25% | 28% |
| 可再生能源 Renewables | 10% | 32% |
| 核能 Nuclear | 10% | 10% |

---

**P-1（Overview）：** 用 `Overall,` 开头，写 2 句概括两张图最主要的特征。**不写任何数字。**
> 提示：① 2000年谁占主导？ ② 到2020年，最大的变化是什么？

```
Overall, it is clear that coal acounted for the largest share, which showed that traditional energy was dominant at that period. However, renewables increased significantly and ranged the top 1 in 2020.
```

---

**P-2（数据句）：** 描述可再生能源从 2000 到 2020 的变化（用 `rose from...to...`）

```
Renewables rose significantly from 10% in 2000 to 32% in 2020.
```

---

**P-3（数据句）：** 描述煤炭的变化（用 `Although X remained..., its share fell from...to...`）

```
Although coal remained the largest share in 2000, its share fell from 55% to 30% in 2020.
```

- [ ] "accounts for" 后用逗号 + at Y%？（Coal accounted for 55%, at the highest share）
- [ ] P-2、P-3 用了过去时（rose / fell / declined）？

## Day 1 复习答案汇总

> 做完复习部分后再看。

### 复习 A 答案

**RA1.** "Rapid urbanization **has had** a negative effect on the environment, which leads to serious air pollution."
（effect 搭配 have，不是 make；natural environment 可省略 natural；the 不可少）

**RA2.** "Due to **the development of** renewable energy, governments have managed to reduce their dependence on fossil fuels."
（Due to 后必须接名词短语；develop → the development of）

**RA3.** "Many elderly workers are **anxious about** losing their pensions and are reluctant to **return** to work."
（anxious about = 担心；return back → return，back 冗余）

---

### 复习 B 答案（拼写）

**RB1.** **losing**（lose → losing；只有一个 s）
**RB2.** **pension fund**（fund = 基金；found = 发现/创立，完全不同的词）
**RB3.** **imbalanced**（im- 前缀 + balanced；im- 用于 b/m/p 开头的词根前）
**RB4.** **citizens**（cit-i-zens；注意 -izen 不是 -izien）
**RB5.** **automation**（自动化过程/趋势；automatic = 自动的，形容词）

---

### 记录希望掌握的单词或知识点

- **have an effect on**（对……有影响）/ **have an impact on** / **affect**（动词）
- **Due to + 名词短语**：due to the adoption of / due to the rapid growth of
- **Unless**（除非）：从句用肯定形式，本身含否定意义
- **accounts for**（占比）后跟 at Y%：Coal accounts for 55%, **at** the highest level.
- 饼图：account for / represent / make up / constitute（均可描述占比）
- **automation**（名词）/ **automated**（形容词）/ **automate**（动词）
