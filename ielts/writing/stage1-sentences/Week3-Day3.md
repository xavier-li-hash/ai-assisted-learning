### 本次高频错误

```
① 主谓一致（Task 2 解释句）
   ❌ when public transport become more access to most citizens
   ✅ when public transport becomes more accessible to most citizens
   → transport 是单数不可数名词，动词用 becomes；
     "access to"（名词短语）此处应用形容词 accessible

② 主谓一致（Task 2 例子句）
   ❌ the government have constructed
   ✅ the government has constructed
   → 集合名词 government 在英式/雅思写作中通常视为单数

③ 形容词误用（Task 2 例子句）
   ❌ a merely public transport system
   ✅ a comprehensive / well-developed public transport system
   → merely = 仅仅（副词，修饰动词/形容词）；这里需要形容词描述"完善的"

④ 关系从句主谓一致
   ❌ which help them save considerable expenditure
   ✅ which helps them save considerable expenditure
   → which 指代 the system（单数），谓语用 helps

⑤ 小结句缺逗号
   ❌ As a result the traffic congestion is alleviated
   ✅ As a result, the traffic congestion is alleviated
   → 句首副词短语后需加逗号

⑥ Task 1 Overview 用词不当
   ❌ ranged the top 1 in 2020
   ✅ became the leading energy source in 2020 / ranked first in 2020
   → range 不能这样用；用 rank first 或 become the leading...

⑦ Task 1 Overview 拼写
   ❌ acounted     ✅ accounted
```

## Day 2 复习答案汇总

> 做完复习部分后再看。

### 复习 A 答案

**RA1.** "When public transport **becomes** more **accessible** to citizens, they tend to choose it over driving."
（transport 单数 → becomes；access 名词 → accessible 形容词）

**RA2.** "As a result**, the government has built** a comprehensive system, which **helps** millions of commuters every day."
（As a result 后加逗号；which 指代单数 system → helps）

**RA3.** "Overall, renewable energy **ranked first / became the leading energy source** in 2020, while coal **accounted for** the largest share in 2000."
（ranged the top 1 → ranked first；acounted → accounted）

---

### 复习 B 答案（拼写）

**RB1.** **accessible**（ac-ces-si-ble；access 的形容词形式）
**RB2.** **comprehensive**（com-pre-hen-sive；意为"全面的、综合的"）
**RB3.** **account for**（account for + 百分比/数量，描述占比）
**RB4.** **commuters**（com-mut-ers；每天上下班的人）
**RB5.** **alleviate**（al-le-vi-ate；alleviate traffic congestion 缓解交通拥堵）

### 记录希望掌握的单词或知识点

- **accessible**（形容词）/ **access to**（名词短语）/ **access**（动词：to access information）三种用法区分
- **government** 雅思写作中视为单数：the government has / invests / plans
- **comprehensive** / **well-developed** / **efficient**——描述基础设施的形容词
- **account for**（占……比例）/ **make up**（构成）/ **represent**（代表）——Task 1 数据描述同义替换
- **alleviate** / **reduce** / **ease**——缓解问题的动词组
- 柱状图对比句型：X was significantly higher than Y, **at** 72% **compared to** 58%.
