### 一、本次高频错误（5分原句 vs 6.5分改写）

---

**错误① Comma Splice（再次出现——最高频！）**

| | 句子 |
|---|---|
| ❌ 5分 | `there are many different requirements in a modern city, it is not possible to force them giving up driving` |
| ✅ 6.5分 | `modern cities have diverse transportation needs; it is neither practical nor necessary to force all residents to abandon driving` |

→ 两个完整句子只用逗号连接 = Comma Splice。修复方法：① 分号；② 改分词短语（making it impossible to...）

---

**错误② 词性误用（beneficial 是形容词，不是名词）**

| | 句子 |
|---|---|
| ❌ 5分 | `upgrading roads has significant beneficial and cannot been igored` |
| ✅ 6.5分 | `expanding road networks has significant benefits and cannot be overlooked` |

→ `beneficial`（形容词：有益的）≠ `benefit/benefits`（名词：益处）
→ `cannot been` → `cannot be`（情态动词后用原形 be）

---

**错误③ force 搭配错误**

| | 句子 |
|---|---|
| ❌ 5分 | `force then giving up driving` |
| ✅ 6.5分 | `force residents to give up driving` |

→ `force sb. to do sth.`（不定式，不是动名词）；`then` → `them`（拼写）

---

**错误④ Conclusion 立场不清晰**

| | 句子 |
|---|---|
| ❌ 5分 | `I believe that both of them should be planed and built better, but only need to balanced better` |
| ✅ 6.5分 | `I believe that prioritising public transport is the more effective long-term solution, as it addresses both congestion and environmental concerns more comprehensively` |

→ Discussion 题的 Conclusion 必须明确说出"我倾向哪一方"，不能模糊地说"都很好"
→ `planed` → `planned`；`need to balanced` → `need to be balanced`

---

**错误⑤ Task 1：illustrates that + 名词 结构错**

| | 句子 |
|---|---|
| ❌ 5分 | `The bar chart illustrates that the enrollment rate for universities sorting by five countries` |
| ✅ 6.5分 | `The bar chart illustrates the university enrollment rates in five countries between 2005 and 2020` |

→ `illustrates` 直接跟名词短语，不用 `that`；`sorting by` → `in`

---

**错误⑥ 冠词（a vs an）**

| | 句子 |
|---|---|
| ❌ 5分 | `all the countries showed a upward trend` |
| ✅ 6.5分 | `all five countries showed an upward trend` |

→ 元音音素开头的单词（upward, increase, overall）前用 `an`

---

**错误⑦ 句子碎片（While 不能独立成句）**

| | 句子 |
|---|---|
| ❌ 5分 | `China rose significantly from 25% to 55%. While the rates in India were consistently smallest...` |
| ✅ 6.5分 | `China recorded the most dramatic growth, rising from 25% to 55%, while India's rate, though the lowest overall, more than doubled from 15% to 32%.` |

→ `While` 引导让步从句，不能独立成句（没有主句就是碎片句）

---

**错误⑧ 复习 RA2 没有完全改正（关系从句主谓一致）**

| | 句子 |
|---|---|
| ❌ 学生答案 | `which help millions of commuters every day` |
| ✅ 正确 | `which helps millions of commuters every day` |

→ `which` 指代 `the system`（单数），谓语用 `helps`。上次已讲，这次仍漏改。

### 三、6.5 分全篇范文（公共交通 vs 道路投资）

> 重点展示：定语从句（which/that）、让步从句（Although/While）的使用

---

**Introduction**
> The question of whether governments should invest in public transport or road infrastructure has sparked considerable debate. While some argue that expanding road networks is essential for individual mobility, I believe that prioritising public transport investment represents a more sustainable and equitable solution.

---

**Body 1（支持公共交通）**
> Those who support greater investment in public transport argue that it is the most effective way to reduce urban congestion. This is because an efficient metro or bus system can carry thousands of passengers simultaneously, which **significantly reduces** the number of private cars on the road. For example, cities such as Singapore and Tokyo, **which have invested heavily in rail networks**, have managed to keep traffic congestion at manageable levels despite their high population density. Therefore, well-funded public transport systems benefit not only individual commuters but also the environment.

---

**Body 2（支持道路建设）**
> However, investing in road infrastructure also plays a vital role in serving those who rely on personal vehicles. This is because modern cities have diverse transportation demands; it is neither practical nor necessary to force all residents to abandon driving. For instance, companies with logistics requirements, parents **who need to commute with young children**, and emergency services such as ambulances all depend heavily on private vehicles. Therefore, maintaining and expanding road networks has significant benefits that cannot be overlooked.

---

**Conclusion**
> In conclusion, while both public transport and road expansion offer genuine benefits, they serve different segments of the population. However, I believe that prioritising public transport is the more effective long-term strategy, as it addresses congestion, pollution, and social equity more comprehensively **than any road-building programme could**.

### 6分写作工具箱（固定用）

```
【Conclusion 公式——3步】
① In conclusion, + 总结双方观点（1句，用 while 连接）
② However, I believe that + 明确立场（1句，说清楚倾向哪方）
③ ...because / as + 理由（1句，说明为什么）

【Comma Splice 三大修复方法】
方法1：换成分号：...; it is...
方法2：加连词：..., and / but / so it is...
方法3：改分词短语：..., making it... / resulting in...

【让步从句句型（Conclusion 加分）】
Although both approaches have merits, I believe [立场], because...
While [对立观点] has some validity, [你的立场] is ultimately more effective.
```

### 记录希望掌握的单词或知识点

- **Comma Splice 三法**：① 分号 `;`；② 加连词 `and/but/so`；③ 分词短语 `making it... / resulting in...`
- **beneficial（adj.）vs benefit（n.）vs benefit（v.）**：
  - adj: This is **beneficial** to public health.
  - n: This has significant **benefits** for public health.
  - v: This will **benefit** public health.
- **cannot be + 过去分词**（被动语态）：cannot be **ignored** / **denied** / **overlooked**
- **prioritise**（英式拼写）/ **prioritize**（美式）——Conclusion 表立场高频词
- **双饼图核心句型**：
  - `X accounted for the largest share at Y%, while Z made up only W%.`
  - `By 2020, renewables had risen dramatically to X%, overtaking coal as the dominant source.`
  - `Oil remained stable at X% throughout the period.`
