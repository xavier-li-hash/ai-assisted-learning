## 【核心规则卡片】先背这3条，贯穿全天

```
规则 1：每个句子必须有【主语 + 谓语动词】
  ❌ Compared to Japan, even though it grew fast, just reaching 20%.
  ✅ Japan, by contrast, grew fast, reaching 20% by 2020.

规则 2：due to 后面只能接【名词/名词短语】，不能接句子
  ❌ due to it saves time
  ✅ due to time savings  （或改用 because it saves time）

规则 3：even though / although 是"让步从句"，必须有主句
  ❌ Although social media connects people, also causes loneliness.
  ✅ Although social media connects people, it also causes loneliness.
```

- 保留 due to: Remote work has become popular due to **time savings on commuting**.

**C3.** Due to **a lack of public transport**, many residents rely on private cars.

（或：due to **budget overruns**）

### 句型工具箱（今日新增）

```
【对比句（替代碎片式 Compared to...）】
- X, by contrast, grew far more slowly, reaching only Y by [year].
- While X maintained the highest level, Y experienced the most dramatic growth.
- Unlike X, which remained stable, Y declined sharply.

【due to 正确用法】
- due to + 名词：due to budget constraints / due to lack of funding
- because + 句子：because the government cut funding
- owing to + 名词（正式）：owing to a rise in demand

【让步从句（although/despite）】
- Although [从句], [主句].
- Despite [名词/动名词], [主句].
  ❌ Despite the economy improved → ✅ Despite the improvement in the economy / Despite the economy improving
```
