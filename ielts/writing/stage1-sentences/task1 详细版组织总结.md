---

## Task 1 字数问题的根源

```
Task 1要求最少150字，理想160-180字

字数不足的原因：
❌ Overview只写一句
❌ Body只写起点和终点，跳过中间变化
❌ 没有做比较
❌ 数字表达单一，没有多样化
```

**解决方案：每个段落都有固定的"字数保障句"**

---

## 字数保障的核心技巧

在开始模板之前，记住这三个**万能扩展句型**，任何图表都能用：

**技巧1 — 加入比较句（每段至少一句）**
```
This figure was significantly higher/lower
than that of [对比对象], which stood at [数字].
```

**技巧2 — 加入差距描述句**
```
The gap between [A] and [B] was [数字],
with [A] recording nearly twice/three times
the figure of [B].
```

**技巧3 — 加入变化幅度句**
```
Overall, [主语] increased/decreased
by [数字] over the period,
representing a [倍数] increase/decrease
from its starting figure.
```

## 一、折线图 Line Graph
**目标字数：165-180字**

```

in/across [地点] from [年份] to [年份].

【Overview — 约40字】
Overall, it is clear that [总体趋势描述].
[最突出特征，如最高/最低/交叉点].

【Body 1 — 约55字】

[主语1] stood at [数字], the highest of all [对象].
This figure [rose/fell] [程度副词]
[throughout/over] the period,
reaching [数字] by [年份].
[主语2], by contrast, began at [数字]
and [变化描述], ending the period at [数字].
The gap between the two [widened/narrowed]
considerably over this time.

【Body 2 — 约55字】
Turning to [第二组数据],
[主语3] started at [数字] in [年份],
which was [higher/lower] than [参照对象].
[It then rose/fell + 程度副词],
[reaching/hitting] a [peak/low] of [数字]
in [年份] before [levelling off/continuing to rise/fall].

[making it the highest/lowest of the three].
```

# 📋 字数达标检查清单

每次写完Task 1，用这个清单自查：

```
Introduction    20-30字   ☐
Overview        35-45字   ☐
Body 1          50-60字   ☐
Body 2          50-60字   ☐
─────────────────────────
总计            155-195字 ☐

字数保障句检查：
☐ Overview写了两个特征（不只一句）
☐ 每个Body段有比较句
☐ 每个Body段有差距描述或倍数表达
☐ 数字表达多样化（不只用"rose to"）
☐ 关键数据点：起点+中间+终点（折线图）
```

## 最容易补充字数的五个句型

当你发现字数不够时，加这些句子：

```
1. 差距句：
"The gap between [A] and [B] stood at
[数字], with [A] recording nearly
[倍数] the figure of [B]."

2. 合并句：
"Together, [A] and [B] accounted for
[合计]% of the total."

3. 排名句：
"This made [主语] the [highest/second largest/
lowest] of all [categories/countries]."

4. 变化幅度句：
"Overall, [主语] increased/decreased
by [数字] percentage points/units
over the period."

5. 总结比较句：
"By the end of the period,
[主语1] was considerably [higher/lower]
than [主语2], despite starting
at a [similar/lower/higher] level."
```
