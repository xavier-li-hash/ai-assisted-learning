## 为什么模板Body段不够用？

模板只是**骨架**，真正的Band 6.5 Body段需要：

```
骨架（模板）    → 知道写什么
肌肉（展开）    → 知道怎么写深
```

一个完整的Body段大约需要**100-120字**，而模板只给你约50字的框架。

## Body段的三种展开方式

### 展开方式一：解释链（最常用）

不只解释一次，而是**层层递进解释**：

```
观点 → 解释1（为什么）→ 解释2（这意味着什么）→ 例子 → 连回主题
```

**例子（科技让生活更复杂）：**

❌ **浅层版（约40字）：**
> "One advantage of technology is that it saves time. This is because people can complete tasks faster. For example, online shopping is convenient."

✅ **深层版（约100字）：**
> "One significant advantage of technology is that it dramatically increases efficiency in daily life. This is because digital tools allow people to complete tasks that previously required hours in a matter of minutes. As a result, individuals have more free time to dedicate to personal development, family, or leisure activities. For example, online banking enables people to manage their finances, pay bills, and transfer money without visiting a physical branch, saving considerable time and effort. This demonstrates that technology, when used effectively, can greatly enhance the quality of everyday life."

---

### 展开方式二：优点+让步（适合Advantages-Disadvantages）

```
观点 → 解释 → 例子 → 承认对立面 → 反驳对立面
```

**例子：**
> "One major advantage of studying abroad is the opportunity to develop cultural awareness and global perspectives. Living in a foreign country exposes students to different customs, languages, and ways of thinking, which broadens their understanding of the world. For instance, a student from China studying in the UK may gain a deeper appreciation of Western approaches to critical thinking and independent learning, skills that are increasingly valued by international employers. While some may argue that this cultural exposure can be achieved through travel alone, the immersive experience of studying and living abroad over an extended period provides a far deeper and more meaningful understanding."

---

### 展开方式三：双论据（最安全，字数最稳定）

```
观点 → 解释1 → 例子1 → 解释2 → 例子2 → 连回主题
```

**例子（贫富差距）：**
> "There are two main reasons why the wealth gap has widened in recent decades. Firstly, the rise of the digital economy has allowed those with capital and technical expertise to accumulate wealth at an unprecedented rate. Technology giants such as Amazon and Google have generated enormous profits for their founders and investors, while ordinary workers in traditional industries have seen little wage growth. Secondly, the increasing cost of urban living has made it increasingly difficult for middle and lower-income groups to save money or invest. Rising housing prices, expensive healthcare, and high education fees consume the majority of ordinary people's income, leaving little room for wealth accumulation."

## 完整Body段公式（推荐版）

把这个公式贴在备考笔记里：

```
句1 — 观点句（Point）
      "One significant [advantage/reason/problem] is that..."

句2 — 第一层解释（Why/How）
      "This is because / This means that..."

句3 — 第二层解释（深化，说明影响或机制）
      "As a result / Consequently / This leads to..."

句4 — 例子（Example）
      "For example / For instance / Take X as an example..."

句5 — 补充细节或让步反驳（可选但加分）
      "While some may argue... / Although... / Furthermore..."

句6 — 连回主题（Link）
      "This demonstrates / Therefore / Hence it is clear that..."
```

## 更新后的完整Body模板

### Task 2 所有题型通用Body模板

**单个论点展开（约100-110字）：**
```
[One significant advantage/reason/problem] is that [观点].
This is because [第一层解释],
which means that [第二层解释/影响].
As a result, [进一步的结果].
For example, [具体例子，越具体越好].
[补充细节，或让步反驳（可选）].
This demonstrates that [连回题目关键词].
```

**双论点展开（约110-120字，适合Problem-Solution）：**
```
There are two main [reasons/solutions/advantages].
Firstly, [论点1].
This is because [解释],
leading to [影响].
For example, [例子].
Secondly, [论点2].
As a result of this, [解释影响].
[具体例子或数据].
Therefore, [连回主题].
```

## 实际对比：同一个Body段，三个质量层次

**题目：** "Technology has made our lives more complicated. To what extent do you agree?"
**Body观点：** 科技确实让生活更复杂

**Band 5版本（约45字）：**
> "Technology makes life complicated. People have to learn many new things. For example, smartphones have many apps that are hard to use. This shows technology is complicated."

**Band 6版本（约80字）：**
> "One reason why technology has made life more complicated is that people are required to constantly learn new skills. As technology evolves rapidly, individuals must regularly update their knowledge to keep up. For example, older generations often struggle to use smartphones or online banking systems. This suggests that while technology offers convenience, it simultaneously creates new challenges for many people."

**Band 6.5版本（约110字）：**
> "One significant reason why technology has made life more complicated is that it demands constant adaptation from users. As digital tools evolve at an unprecedented pace, individuals are required to continuously update their skills simply to perform basic daily tasks. This places a particularly heavy burden on older generations and those with limited access to education, who may find themselves unable to navigate essential services such as online banking or government websites. For example, in many countries, public services have shifted entirely online, leaving digitally illiterate citizens without access to healthcare or financial support. This demonstrates that technological advancement, despite its benefits, can create significant barriers for vulnerable groups in society."

## 关键结论

```
模板 = 知道往哪个方向走
展开 = 真正走到终点

提升字数和深度的三个方法：
1. 加第二层解释（"This means that / As a result"）
2. 让例子更具体（国家名/公司名/数据/研究）
3. 加让步反驳句（"While some may argue..."）
```
