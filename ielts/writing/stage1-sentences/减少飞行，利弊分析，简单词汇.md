
In recent years, more and more people have decided to fly less often or to stop flying completely. Although this change can cause some problems for people and companies, I believe the benefits to the environment are more important. This essay will explain why.

On the one hand, flying less can help the envrionment in important ways. Planes burn large amount of fuel, which puts harmful gases into the air. These gases make the earth warmer, and the problem is getting worse every year. If lower people fly, the amount of these harmful gases would go down, which would help slow down climate change. This is good not just for one country, but for the whole world.

On the other hand, it is not always easy for people to stop flying. For exmaple, if someone has family living another country, flying is often the only way to visit them. For companies, meeting people from other countries in person is sometimes very important for building a good working relationship and getting new business. If people fly less, some of these connections many become harder to maintain.

However, I think these problems are not as serious as they seem. Many meetings that used to need travel now can be done online, useing tools like video calls. This has become very common in recent years and works very well in many situations. Also, the damage that flying does to the environment is a very big and long-term problem thats affect everyone. The difficulties caused by flying less are smaller in comparison and only affect some people.

In conclusion, although flying less may make life harder for some people and companies, I believe that benefits to the environment are more important. Taking care of the planet is something that everyone needs to think about.
