### 三、7分范文（词汇简单易懂）

The bar chart shows the total number of library loans in Little Chalfont from 2007 to 2016, while the pie chart and table provide information about member age groups and loan categories in 2016.

Overall, the total number of loans rose steadily over the period, despite some minor fluctuations. In 2016, adults aged 18 to 64 made up the largest share of members, and fiction was by far the most borrowed category.

Regarding the bar chart, loans stood at just over 15,000 in 2007 and grew steadily, reaching a peak of around 21,000 in 2011. After a slight dip and some fluctuation between 2012 and 2014, the figure recovered and continued to rise gradually in the final three years.

Turning to the 2016 data, adults aged 18 to 64 accounted for the largest proportion of library members at 51%, followed by children at 22%. Young adults aged 13 to 17 and elderly members over 65 made up 15% and 12% respectively. This age pattern is reflected in the borrowing categories: adult fiction and children's fiction were the two most popular types, each at 38%, together representing 76% of all loans. In contrast, children's DVDs and adult audiobooks were the least borrowed, at just 1% and 2% respectively.

### 四、关键差距对比

| 维度 | 你的作文 | 范文 |
|------|---------|------|
| 语法 | 碎片句、时态错误、between...to | 时态统一过去时，句式完整 |
