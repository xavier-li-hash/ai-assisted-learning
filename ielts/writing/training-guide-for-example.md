Introduction

The question of whether governments should invest more in public transport or road infrastructure has long been debated. While expanding road networks may improve individual convenience, prioritising public transport is, in my view, a more sustainable and socially beneficial approach in the long run.

⸻

Body 1（公共交通）

Those who support greater investment in public transport often point out that it is one of the most effective ways to reduce urban congestion. This is mainly because high-capacity systems such as metros and buses can move a large number of people using far less road space per person. As a result, fewer private cars are needed, which helps ease traffic pressure across the entire city rather than just in certain areas. For example, cities like Singapore and Tokyo have developed extensive rail networks, allowing millions of commuters to travel efficiently every day. Consequently, traffic remains relatively controlled despite their dense populations. In addition to reducing congestion, such systems also lower emissions and make cities more environmentally friendly.

⸻

Body 2（道路）

That said, investment in road infrastructure is still necessary, as not all transport needs can be met by public systems. In reality, modern cities rely on a wide range of activities that require flexible and direct travel. For instance, logistics companies need road networks to deliver goods efficiently, while families with young children may find private vehicles more practical for daily travel. Emergency services, in particular, depend entirely on clear and well-maintained roads to respond quickly. Therefore, improving road conditions and expanding capacity where needed remains an important part of urban planning, even if it is not the primary focus.

⸻

Conclusion

In conclusion, although both forms of investment are important, they serve different purposes within a city. However, prioritising public transport offers greater overall benefits, as it not only addresses congestion more effectively but also contributes to environmental protection and social accessibility in ways that road expansion alone cannot achieve.

🔍 这版提升在哪里？

1️⃣ 深度（最关键提升）

不再只是：

reduce congestion ❌

而是：

为什么能减少（capacity / space / system effect）✅

👉 这就是从 7 → 7.5+ 的核心差别

⸻

2️⃣ 连接更自然（不像模板）

替换了一些“考试味很重”的连接词：
	•	This is because → This is mainly because
	•	Therefore → As a result / Consequently / That said

👉 更像真实思考，而不是背结构

⸻

3️⃣ 论证更“整体视角”

比如这一句很关键：

rather than just in certain areas

👉 表达的是：
	•	公共交通是“系统性解决方案”
	•	道路只是“局部缓解”

这是考官很喜欢的分析层次

⸻

4️⃣ 结论更有比较力度

原来：

more effective

现在：

in ways that road expansion alone cannot achieve

👉 明确“对比优势”，更接近 Band 8 写法
